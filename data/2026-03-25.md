Method: 使用包含潮汐作用的N体模拟，研究三种散射机制：热散射（a₁ < 0.1 AU）、暖散射（0.1 < a₁ < 1 AU）和冷散射（1 < a₁ < 10 AU）。通过模拟行星-行星散射过程，分析不同机制产生的行星轨道特征。

Conclusion: 行星-行星散射为热木星和暖木星提供了统一的形成框架。该理论预测：1) 暖木星（特别是a >~ 0.3 AU）不应完全对齐；2) 暖木星应常伴有非零相互倾角（<~ 30度）的近距离伴星。

In this paper, we revisit planet-planet scattering and demonstrate that it provides a unified framework for both populations. Using N-body simulations with tides, we explore three regimes: hot (a_1 < 0.1 AU), warm (0.1 < a_1 < 1 AU), and cold (1 < a_1 < 10 AU) scattering. Hot scattering predominantly produces compact hot-Jupiter pairs, which are rarely observed, implying this channel is rare. Cold scattering readily produces retrograde hot Jupiters and likely constitutes a main reservoir feeding the hot-Jupiter population. However, cold scattering produces few inner warm Jupiters at a at about 0.1-0.3 AU. We show that warm scattering naturally fills this gap: high-inclination inner warm Jupiters produced by warm scattering are preferentially removed through further eccentricity excitation followed by tidal circularization into hot Jupiters. As a result, the surviving inner warm Jupiters are biased toward a broad range of eccentricities but modest inclinations, producing the observed "eccentric-but-aligned" population. This story makes testable predictions: (i) warm Jupiters, especially at a >~ 0.3 AU, should not be exclusively aligned, and (ii) warm Jupiters should often host nearby companions with non-negligible mutual inclinations up to <~ 30 degrees.

*N. Biver,D. Bockelée-Morvan,R. Moreno,J. Crovisier,G. Paubert,V. Zakharov,J. Boissier,M. A. Cordiner,N. X. Roth*

Motivation: 研究星际彗星3I/ATLAS的彗发气体成分，了解其化学组成特征，并与太阳系彗星进行比较，探索星际彗星的起源和演化。

Method: 使用IRAM 30米望远镜在2025年11月1-3日进行毫米波光谱观测，当时彗星距离太阳1.36-1.37天文单位，检测彗发中的气体分子谱线。

Result: 明确检测到HCN、CH3OH、CO、H2CO，CS和CH3CN有约4σ的探测信号，未检测到H2S。CO、H2CO、CH3OH、CH3CN相对于HCN的丰度处于太阳系彗星测量值的上限范围，硫碳比极低，彗发气体膨胀速度异常缓慢。

Conclusion: 3I/ATLAS的化学组成与太阳系彗星存在显著差异，特别是硫碳比极低，膨胀速度缓慢可能由CO2等重分子驱动或冰粒升华贡献大量气体产生，这些特征可能反映了其星际起源或不同的形成环境。

Abstract: 3I/ATLAS is the third interstellar comet identified as passing through the Solar System. Its high outgassing activity and favourable perihelion passage on October 29, 2025 UT provided an excellent opportunity to investigate the composition of its coma gases through millimeter spectroscopy. We present observations undertaken with the IRAM 30-m telescope on November 1--3, 2025 at an heliocentric distance of 1.36--1.37 au. Lines of HCN, CH$_3$OH, CO, and H$_2$CO are well detected, and $\sim$4$σ$ detections are obtained for CS and CH$_3$CN. The search for H$_2$S was unsuccessful. Abundances of CO, H$_2$CO, CH$_3$OH, and CH$_3$CN relative to HCN are in the upper ranges of values measured in Solar System comets. The sulfur-to-carbon abundance ratio in 3I/ATLAS's coma is at most the minimum value observed in comets. The unusually low expansion velocity of coma gases suggests a near-nucleus gas flow driven by heavy molecules such as CO$_2$, and/or a large fraction of the gaseous production coming from subliming icy grains.
