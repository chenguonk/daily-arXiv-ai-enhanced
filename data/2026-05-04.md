### [1] [The dark and featureless surface of rocky exoplanet LHS 3844 b from JWST mid-infrared spectroscopy](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00100)
*Sebastian Zieba,Laura Kreidberg,Brandon P. Coy,Aaron Bello-Arufe,Kimberly Paragas,Xintong Lyu,Renyu Hu,Aishwarya Iyer,Edwin S. Kite,Daniel D. B. Koll,Kay Wohlfarth,Emerson Whittaker,Heather Knutson,Robin Wordsworth,Caroline Morley,Laura Schaefer*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: JWST对 rocky 系外行星 LHS 3844 b 的 5-12 微米热发射光谱分析表明，其表面为暗色、低硅（如玄武岩或富橄榄石）物质，排除了新鲜粉末表面，并限定了 CO₂ 和 SO₂ 气体的痕量上限（100 mbar 和 10 μbar），整体符合古老、经空间风化的表面特征，无火山气体积累证据。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: JWST开启了利用中红外光谱直接表征 rocky 系外行星表面的新时代，不同岩石类型具有独特光谱特征，可揭示行星的地质历史和内部过程。

Method: 利用JWST获取 LHS 3844 b 在 5-12 微米波段的热发射光谱，通过光谱匹配分析表面成分，并约束痕量气体浓度。

Result: 表面最佳匹配为暗色、低硅物质（玄武岩/富橄榄石）；排除新鲜粉末；CO₂ 上限 100 mbar（5σ），SO₂ 上限 10 μbar（3σ）。

Conclusion: LHS 3844 b 表面为古老、经空间风化的表面，无火山气体积累证据，为理解 rocky 行星表面演化提供了关键观测约束。

Abstract: JWST has opened a new era in the study of rocky exoplanets, enabling direct characterization of their surfaces with mid-infrared spectroscopy. Different types of rock have distinct spectral features that are diagnostic of the chemical composition and other physical properties like surface texture. Measurements of these features can provide valuable clues about a planet's geologic history and interior processes. Here we report a JWST 5-12 micron thermal emission spectrum for the rocky exoplanet LHS 3844 b. It is best matched by a dark, low-silica surface, such as basalt or other olivine-rich materials. The spectrum rules out fresh powder surfaces; however, space weathering can darken the powders and make them more consistent with the data. The data also disfavor trace concentrations of CO$_2$ or SO$_2$ gas (with 5-sigma and 3-sigma upper limits of 100 mbar and 10 microbar, respectively). Taken together, these results are well fit by an old, space-weathered surface with no evidence of accumulated volcanic gases.

### [2] [Confirmation of Fe I on MASCARA-5 b's Dayside Observed With EXPRES](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00154)
*James T. Sikora,Joe Llama,Rachael M. Roettenbacher,Elisabeth M. Brann,Jean-Michel Désert,Alex S. Polanski,Malena Rice,Lily Zhao*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本文通过EXPRES@LDT高分辨率光谱仪对超热木星MASCARA-5~b/TOI-1431~b的掩前时域观测，确认了其白天侧存在气体Fe~{\sc i}发射线（5.5σ显著性），证实其大气热剖面倒转（下层~2000K，上层~4500K），并发现掩前信号存在-3.2±1.4 km/s的蓝移，可能由风引起；未探测到Fe~{\sc ii}和Cr~{\sc i}。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 已有研究在掩后相位探测到Fe~{\sc i}发射线，表明MASCARA-5~b具有热倒转大气，但需要更多轨道相位的观测来约束其大气化学、热结构和动力学。

Method: 利用EXPRES@LDT高分辨率光学光谱仪获取掩前时域序列观测数据，通过分析吸收/发射线特征和速度偏移来表征大气成分与动力学。

Result: 1) 以5.5σ置信度确认白天侧Fe~{\sc i}发射线；2) 确认热剖面倒转，下层~2000K，上层~4500K；3) 未探测到Fe~{\sc ii}和Cr~{\sc i}；4) 掩前信号存在-3.2±1.4 km/s的蓝移。

Conclusion: MASCARA-5~b的大气确实存在热倒转，且掩前观测揭示了可能由风引起的系统性蓝移，为进一步研究其大气动力学提供了重要约束。

Abstract: MASCARA-5~b/TOI-1431~b is an ultra-hot Jupiter \citep[$P_{\rm orb}=2.650237\pm0.000003\,{\rm d}$, $T_{\rm eq}=2370\pm70\,{\rm K}$, $M_{\rm p}=3.12\pm0.18\,M_{\rm Jup}$, $R_{\rm p}=1.49\pm0.05\,R_{\rm Jup}$;][]{addison2021} orbiting a bright Am star ($V=8.0\,{\rm mag}$). Recent time-series observations obtained with PEPSI@LBT during the planet's post-eclipse phases have revealed Fe~{\sc i} emission lines indicative of a thermally inverted atmosphere. These observations demonstrate that MASCARA-5~b is well-suited to atmospheric characterization via emission spectroscopy, thereby motivating further follow-up observations covering additional orbital phases to constrain the planet's atmospheric chemistry, thermal structure, and dynamics. Here we present pre-eclipse time-series observations obtained with the high-resolution optical spectrograph EXPRES@LDT. Our analysis confirms the previous detection of gas-phase Fe~{\sc i} on MASCARA-5~b's dayside (with a $5.5σ$ significance obtained from two nights of observations) and the fact that the thermal profile is inverted with lower and upper temperatures $\sim2000\,{\rm K}$ and $\sim4500\,{\rm K}$, respectively. A search for Fe~{\sc ii} and Cr~{\sc i} did not yield any plausible detections. We also find that the pre-eclipse signal exhibits a non-negligible blueshift of $-3.2\pm1.4\,{\rm km/s}$ potentially caused by winds.

### [3] [Smaller Than Earth Habitability Model (STEHM): The Lower Size Limit for Atmosphere Retention in the Habitable Zone](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00170)
*Michelle L. Hill,Stephen R. Kane,Bradford J. Foley,Laura K. Schaefer*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本研究通过STEHM模型模拟发现，在类太阳恒星宜居带中，≥0.8R⊕的星球可长期维持大气层，而更小星球会失去大气；初始碳储量是影响大气保持的最关键因素。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 随着系外行星观测技术进步，发现的行星越来越小，亟需回答：行星小到何种程度仍能维持大气层？这直接关系到宜居行星的搜索范围。

Method: 开发了STEHM模型，模拟0.5-1.0R⊕的停滞盖行星在类太阳恒星宜居带中的长期（数十亿年）大气保持能力，并变化地球-like默认参数进行敏感性分析。

Result: ≥0.8R⊕行星能维持大气，更小则丧失；参数变化仅引起小幅边界调整（部分情况下≥0.7R⊕可保持）；初始碳储量是最关键参数，需要数量级差异才能显著影响大气寿命。

Conclusion: 小行星的大气保持强烈依赖于形成条件和早期演化，为未来岩质系外行星观测及其潜在宜居性提供了重要约束。

Abstract: With recent advances in exoplanet observational techniques enabling the discovery of increasingly smaller planets, a crucial question emerges in the search for habitable planets: how small can a planet be and still maintain an atmosphere? We present results from the Smaller Than Earth Habitability Model (STEHM) which examines how small a planet can be and still maintain a long-term (multi-gigayear) atmosphere for planets from 1.0$R_\oplus$ down to 0.5$R_\oplus$. The model is based on a stagnant lid planet orbiting within the habitable zone of a sun-like star. Our model demonstrates that planets $\geq$0.8$R_\oplus$ can maintain their atmospheres under our Earth-like default conditions for a solar analog star, while smaller planets lose their atmospheres. Variations from the default Earth-like values cause mostly minor variations to the planet size boundary results, with some changes allowing $\geq$0.7$R_\oplus$ planets to maintain their atmosphere. Initial carbon inventory emerges as the most influential parameter for atmospheric retention, though orders of magnitude difference to Earth values are required to make a significant difference to longevity of atmospheric retention. Planets with substantial initial carbon content, large amounts of heat producing elements, cool initial mantle temperatures and low core radius fractions show the best atmospheric retention capabilities. Our results indicate that atmospheric retention on small planets depends strongly on their formation conditions and early evolution, providing important constraints for future observations of rocky exoplanets and their potential habitability.

### [4] [ExoplaNeT accRetion mOnitoring sPectroscopic surveY (ENTROPY) III. Optical He I line profiles of the accreting super Jupiter Delorme 1 (AB)b](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00289)
*Gayathri Viswanath,Mickaël Bonnefoy,Catherine Dougados,Simon C. Ringqvist,Markus Janson,Dorian Demars,Aurora Sicilia-Aguilar,Jérôme Bouvier,Gabriel-Dominique Marleau,Evelyne Alecian,Gaël Chauvin*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本研究首次对行星质量天体Delorme 1 (AB)b的氦发射线进行高分辨率光谱分析，发现其与经典T Tauri星相似但更窄的不对称线轮廓，表明发射主要来源于激波后区，吸积过程主导了氦辐射。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 高分辨率氦发射线光谱是研究经典T Tauri星吸积几何的有力工具，但在行星质量天体中尚缺乏类似研究。

Method: 利用33个高信噪比VLT/UVES光谱（R~50,000），分析Delorme 1 (AB)b的7条He I线（>5σ），测量线轮廓不对称性、速度偏移及氦线光度，推算吸积光度与吸积率。

Result: 探测到7条He I线，其中4条呈现不对称双峰结构（窄成分~0 km/s，宽成分红移~15 km/s）；吸积光度为1.3e-5 L☉，吸积率为0.7e-8 MJup/yr，略高于紫外过量法估值。

Conclusion: Delorme 1 (AB)b的He I线轮廓与经典T Tauri星类似但宽度更小，窄成分起源于行星表面附近的激波后区，宽成分靠近激波前沿；氦发射主要由吸积驱动，可能存在色球活动贡献。

Abstract: High-resolution spectroscopic observations of helium emission lines provide a powerful probe of accretion geometry in classical T Tauri stars, revealing regions not well traced by hydrogen lines. Parallel studies in the planetary-mass regime are lacking. In this work, we investigate helium emission from the nearby (47 pc), wide-orbit (~84 au), ~13 $M_{Jup}$ accreting circumbinary companion Delorme 1 (AB)b and use resolved line profiles to constrain their origin. We analyse 33 high-S/N VLT/UVES spectra spanning near-ultraviolet to optical wavelengths at R~50,000. We detect seven He I lines at >5$σ$ confidence - 3890, 4027, 4473, 4923, 5017, 5877, and 6680 Ȧ - with significant epoch-to-epoch variability. The He I 5877, 4923, 4473, and 4027 Ȧ lines are asymmetric, showing a narrow component near 0 km/s and a broad component redshifted by ~15 km/s. The accretion luminosity ($1.3^{+1.6}_{-0.7}\times 10^{-5} L_{\odot}$) and mass accretion rate ($0.7^{+0.9}_{-0.4} \times 10^{-8} M_{Jup} yr^{-1}$) inferred from the median He I line luminosities are broadly consistent with, but slightly higher than, estimates from the ultraviolet excess. We conclude that protoplanet Delorme 1 (AB)b shows asymmetric He I profiles analogous to those of classical T Tauri stars, but with much smaller narrow- and broad-component widths. The triplet-singlet line ratio, a strong correlation with ultraviolet excess and the near-zero, redshifted velocities obtained for the narrow component suggest that it originates within the post-shock region, close to the planet surface. The persistent redshift of the broad component, its line width, and velocity correlation with the narrow component imply an origin within the shock structure, closer to the shock front. Emission seems to be dominated by accretion based on the obtained accretion luminosities, but a contribution from chromospheric activity may be present.

### [5] [The Rocky Planet Picture Show: Implementation of Surface Reflection and Emission in $\texttt{POSEIDON}$ with Application to and Interpretation of JWST Data](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00700)
*Elijah Mullens,Ryan J. MacDonald,Marina E. Gemma,Ishan Mishra,Esteban Gazel,Nikole K. Lewis*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本文在开源检索代码POSEIDON(v1.4)中实现了同时考虑行星表面发射/反射与大气吸收/散射的新功能。通过分析，发现真实岩石表面会在发射光谱中产生中红外诊断吸收特征、大气透明窗伪特征以及表面-大气界面伪温度反转导致的吸收特征翻转。研究证明JWST现有数据可区分稀薄与浓厚大气，并探索了JWST MIRI LRS约束表面地质的能力，同时提供了实验室测量的表面反照率开源数据库。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 当前JWST和未来HWO等观测计划将聚焦于岩质系外行星的表面特征刻画，但现有模型未能充分考虑真实岩石表面（波长依赖的反照率）与大气之间的复杂相互作用，亟需发展能够同时解析表面发射/反射与大气吸收/散射的完整检索框架。

Method: 在开源检索代码POSEIDON v1.4中实现了发射+反射表面与吸收+散射大气的一体化模型；利用实验室测量的波长依赖方向-半球反射率数据作为表面反照率输入；对JWST实际观测数据（TOI-1685b和55 Cancri e）进行大气+表面联合检索；对合成数据集测试JWST MIRI LRS区分花岗岩与玄武岩表面的能力。

Result: (1) 真实岩石表面通过三种机制影响发射光谱：中红外诊断吸收特征、大气透明窗伪特征、表面-大气界面伪温度反转导致的吸收特征翻转；(2) JWST现有数据可区分≤1 bar（稀薄）与≥0.1 bar（浓厚）大气；(3) 足够信噪比下JWST MIRI LRS可区分花岗岩与玄武岩表面；(4) 建立了开源的地质分类实验室表面反照率数据库。

Abstract: The surface characterization of rocky exoplanets via emission spectroscopy represents a frontier of current (JWST) and future (HWO) observational efforts. Here, we implement new features in the open-source retrieval code $\texttt{POSEIDON (v1.4)}$ to fully account for an emitting and reflecting planetary surface and an overlying absorbing and scattering atmosphere. We show that realistic rocky surfaces (with wavelength-dependent albedos derived from laboratory measurements) affect emission spectra by imparting mid-infrared diagnostic absorption features, imprinting pseudo-features due to atmospheric transparency windows, and flipping absorption features to emission via surface-atmosphere interface pseudo-temperature inversions. We demonstrate that current JWST spectral data can distinguish between tenuous (low surface pressure, $\leq$ 1 bar) and thick (high surface pressures, $\geq$ 0.1 bar) atmospheres by performing atmosphere + surface retrievals on published JWST emission data of the rocky worlds TOI-1685b and 55 Cancri e. We then explore JWST MIRI LRS's capability to constrain surface geology of rocky worlds, finding that with sufficient SNR retrievals can distinguish between granite-like and basaltic surfaces for synthetic datasets. Finally, we provide an open-source database of lab-derived surface albedos (in the form of directional-hemispherical reflectances), organized by geologic classification and include supplemental tables developed to foster future collaboration between geology and exoplanet science. Our atmosphere + surface retrieval technique provides a pathway to probe geologic processes on rocky exoplanets, showing that upcoming JWST data for terrestrial worlds will enable a deeper exploration of rocky surfaces beyond our Solar System.
