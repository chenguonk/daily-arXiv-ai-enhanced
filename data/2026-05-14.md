### [1] [$αβq_\mathrm{th}$-mapping of planet-induced density wave damping in protoplanetary discs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12607)
*Amelia J. Cordwell,Roman R. Rafikov*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本论文通过大量流体动力学模拟，研究了行星在原行星盘中激发的密度波如何因粘性、冷却以及非线性波演化而发生阻尼，并发现非线性激波形成通常是最主要的角动量沉积机制，而粘性阻尼效率较低，冷却在低质量行星情况下重要性接近非线性效应。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为了正确解释原行星盘（以及其他天体物理盘）中的亚结构观测结果，需要深入理解行星激发密度波的角动量如何沉积到盘流体中，即密度波的不同阻尼机制（粘性、冷却、非线性演化）的相对重要性。

Method: 开展大量流体动力学模拟，系统改变粘性参数（α）、冷却时标（β）和行星质量（q_th），从模拟中提取波的角动量沉积径向分布，定量评估不同阻尼机制的效率作为行星质量、粘性和冷却时间的联合函数。

Result: 非线性波演化（激波形成）通常是最主要的角动量沉积机制；对于低质量行星（q_th<1），冷却时标接近局部轨道时标的冷却机制重要性可与非线性效应相当；线性粘性阻尼效率较低，需α≳10^{-1.5}才能显著影响热质量行星的波阻尼，即使最低质量行星（q_th=0.025）也需α≳10^{-2.9}。

Conclusion: 研究结果可用于解释原行星盘观测，并对理解其他天体物理盘中的波传播具有重要意义。

Abstract: Planets embedded in protoplanetary discs are capable of creating a wide variety of substructures through gravitational interactions. This process is mediated through the excitation and damping of density waves which carry angular momentum across the disc. Therefore, to interpret observations of substructures, it is critical to understand the physical processes which lead to deposition of wave angular momentum to the disc fluid. In this study, we explore the relative efficiency of viscosity ($α$), cooling ($β$), and non-linear wave evolution ($q_\mathrm{th}$) in damping planet-generated density waves. We run a large suite of hydrodynamic simulations varying viscosity, cooling timescale, and planetary mass, from which we extract radial profiles of wave angular momentum deposition. We quantify the efficiency of different wave damping mechanisms as a joint function of planetary mass, viscosity and cooling time. We find that nonlinear wave evolution leading to shock formation is typically the most important cause of angular momentum deposition, but that cooling on timescales comparable to local orbital time reaches similar levels of importance for low mass planets (sub-thermal, $q_\mathrm{th}<1$). On the contrary, linear wave damping due to viscosity is rather inefficient, requiring $α\gtrsim 10^{-1.5}$ to noticeably affect damping of waves launched by thermal mass planets. Even for lowest mass planets considered ($q_\mathrm{th}=0.025$), viscosity affects wave damping only if $α\gtrsim 10^{-2.9}$. Our findings could be applied to interpret observations of protoplanetary discs; they are also important for understanding wave propagation in other types of astrophysical discs.

### [2] [Extensions of Brown Hamiltonian-III. Applications to irregular satellites of giant planets](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12864)
*Hanlun Lei,Xiaoyan Leng,Evgeni Grishin*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本文在扩展Brown Hamiltonian框架基础上，引入修正的Lidov积分（$C_{\rm ZLK}$）作为诊断指标，证明当$C_{\rm ZLK} < 0$时卫星被困在von Zeipel–Lidov–Kozai（ZLK）共振中。通过对已知不规则卫星群应用该判据，识别出27颗候选天体，N体模拟验证了其中26颗，唯一例外S/2019 S1归因于其靠近分界线。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 不规则卫星受强烈太阳扰动影响，长期轨道预测极为困难，现有方法缺乏高效的诊断指标来识别ZLK共振状态。

Method: 基于Paper I的扩展Brown Hamiltonian框架，引入修改的Lidov积分$C_{\rm ZLK}$作为诊断指标；通过$C_{\rm ZLK} < 0$判据识别ZLK共振中的不规则卫星；使用直接N体模拟验证预测结果。

Result: 从已知不规则卫星中识别出27颗ZLK共振候选天体，N体模拟验证26例正确，仅S/2019 S1因靠近分界线而出现偏差。

Conclusion: $C_{\rm ZLK}$可作为识别ZLK共振的关键参数，为分析弱层级三体系统的长期动力学提供了高效工具。

Abstract: Irregular satellites, orbiting at large distances from their host planets, are subject to strong solar perturbations that render long-term orbital predictions particularly challenging. Building upon the extended Brown Hamiltonian framework developed in Paper I, we introduce the modified Lidov integral ($C_{\rm ZLK}$) as a practical diagnostic index to characterize the dynamical modes. We demonstrate that a satellite is trapped inside the von Zeipel--Lidov--Kozai (ZLK) resonance when $C_{\rm ZLK} < 0$. Applying this criterion to the known population of irregular satellites, we identify 27 candidates in libration. Direct $N$-body simulations confirm 26 of these predictions, with the sole exception of S/2019 S1, whose discrepancy is attributed to its proximity to the separatrix. These results establish $C_{\rm ZLK}$ as a decisive parameter for identifying the ZLK resonance, providing an efficient tool for analyzing the secular dynamics in weakly hierarchical three-body systems.

### [3] [Fizzy water ice in space: CO$_2$ adsorption, binding energies and its fate in a protoplanetary disk](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13308)
*Alicja Bulik,V. Bariosco,E. Mates-Torres,P. Ugliengo,K. Furuya,C. Ceccarelli,A. Rimola*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 该论文通过计算CO₂在非晶水冰上的结合能分布（双峰高斯分布），并评估其对原行星盘中气固分配和光谱特征的影响，发现结合能分布和指前因子显著影响雪线位置和气体比例的分布范围。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: CO₂是星际冰中第三丰富的成分，其在水冰上的吸附特性（结合能）对精确的星际化学模型和行星形成过程中的化学成分传承至关重要，但需要更准确的结合能分布数据。

Method: 采用ACO-FROST程序计算CO₂在非晶水冰冰粒模型多个吸附位点上的结合能分布，使用ONIOM方案计算结合能，并通过Polanyi-Wigner关系估算指前因子。

Result: CO₂结合能呈双峰高斯分布（μ₁=1648K, σ₁=229K, μ₂=2339K, σ₂=274K）；覆盖模拟显示CO₂在水冰表面具有非润湿特性；结合能分布使气体比例更加扩展，指前因子显著影响雪线位置。

Conclusion: 使用结合能分布而非单一值来模拟原行星盘中CO₂的气固分馏更为准确，指前因子对雪线位置和气相比例有重要影响，这对精确的星际化学模型和行星形成研究具有重要意义。

Abstract: CO2 is the third most abundant ice component found on dust grains in star-forming regions and a common ingredient of exoplanet atmospheres. Characterization of its adsorption properties on ices through the binding energy (BE) is essential for accurate astrochemical modelling and understanding chemical inheritance in planet formation. We aim to derive an accurate BE distribution of CO2 on water ices. Our goal is to understand the impact of the BE distribution on the abundance of gaseous and frozen CO2 in a generic protoplanetary disk and the spectral absorption features of frozen CO2. The ACO-FROST procedure is used for computing the BE distribution, where CO2 molecules are adsorbed on several sites of an amorphous water ice grain model. The BEs are computed using an ONIOM scheme. The BEs of CO2 follow a bimodal Gaussian distribution characterised by the following parameters: μ1 = 1648K, σ1=229K, μ2=2339K, σ2=274K.For each BE bin, the pre-exponential factor was estimated using two models and the Polanyi-Wigner relationship. Comparison with previous studies, both experimental and computational, show good agreement on the range of the BEs. The impact of the adsorption on water ice on the spectral features of CO2 molecule is evaluated. The coverage simulation shows the non-wetting properties of CO2 on the water ice surface. We discuss the impact of using a BE distribution and different pre-exponential factors to calculate the partitioning between the ice and gas in a generic protoplanetary disk. We confirm that the use of BE distribution to model the gas and ice fractionation in a protoplanetary disk causes the gas fraction to be significantly more extended. Furthermore, we show that the prefactor has a significant impact on where the snowline forms and on the final extent of the gas fraction in the disk.

### [4] [Comet 1P/Halley Completes 15 Orbits in 1,151 Years: Commensurability with the Solar System Quasi-Period and Evidence for Jupiter-Saturn Dynamical Coupling](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13409)
*Carlos Baiget Orts*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本文发现哈雷彗星的轨道周期（76.713年）与1,151年行星准周期T*精确共振（比值15.004，角残差仅+1.43度），是所有太阳系天体中最小偏差。木星通过相位调制、土星通过距离-振幅调制共同维持其长期周期稳定性，使15个轨道周期后的累积偏差仅为随机游走期望的9.4%。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探究哈雷彗星是否参与1,151年的行星准周期T*，并理解其轨道周期在混沌与长期稳定之间的矛盾。

Method: 分析2,225年间（30次回归）的历史近日点记录，计算平均轨道周期与T*的比值；运用四种统计检验验证木星和土星对哈雷彗星周期的调制机制（相位调制、距离-振幅调制）；通过随机游走对比评估周期偏差累积。

Result: 哈雷彗星以15.004的比值参与T*，角残差仅+1.43度（p=0.009）；木星相位调制显著（p=0.027-0.04），土星距离-振幅调制显著（r=-0.496, p=0.007）；15个周期累积偏差仅为随机游走期望的9.4%。

Conclusion: 哈雷彗星的个体轨道混沌（Lyapunov时间~70年）与千年尺度的平均周期稳定性并不矛盾：木星和土星在T*基线尺度上相干地抵消扰动，使平均周期在千年尺度上保持稳定。

Abstract: I investigate whether comet 1P/Halley participates in the 1,151-year planetary quasi-period T* identified in a companion paper (Baiget Orts 2026a, arXiv:2604.03049). Using historical perihelion records spanning 2,225 years (30 apparitions, 239 BCE to 1986 CE), I find that Halley's mean orbital period P_bar = 76.713 yr satisfies T*/P_bar = 15.004, yielding an angular residue of +1.43 degrees -- the smallest of any Solar System body examined, including all seven planets that participate in T* (Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, and Neptune; p = 0.009). No other Halley-type comet participates: all examined HTCs exhibit residues of 80--130 degrees, comparable to Uranus (108 degrees), the sole planetary non-participant. Four independent statistical tests establish that Jupiter and Saturn couple to Halley's orbital period through distinct mechanisms. Jupiter acts through phase-dependent modulation: its angular position at each perihelion predicts the period deviation (p = 0.027--0.04, three methods). Saturn acts through distance-amplitude modulation: closer approaches produce larger deviations regardless of sign (r = -0.496, p = 0.007), specific to Saturn's actual orbital phase (random-phase control p = 0.133). After 15 orbits, the cumulative period deviation is only 9.4% of the random-walk expectation -- direct evidence of coherent perturbation cancellation over one T* cycle. The orbit-to-orbit chaos (Lyapunov time ~70 yr) and the long-term mean stability are not contradictory: the same Jupiter-Saturn forces that cause individual-orbit variability cancel coherently over the T* baseline, anchoring the mean period at the millennium scale.

### [5] [Global evolution of electric fields during planet encircling dust storms on Mars](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13781)
*Ina Taxis,Leonardos Gkouvelis,Richard A. Urata,Melinda A. Kahre,Amanda S. Brecht*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本文利用火星全球气候模型耦合摩擦起电与静电诊断方案，研究了全球沙尘暴期间电场产生与演化机制。模拟显示：南部中低纬度带存在剧烈放电活动，近地表电场强度达10²–10³ V/m，电场受沙尘载荷、湍流碰撞和电导率共同调控，且昼夜电导率变化显著抑制白天电场积累。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 全球沙尘暴从根本上重塑火星天气和近地表静电环境，但目前对风暴期间电场生成与演化机制缺乏定量理解，需要建立框架以识别放电高发区并指导未来任务设计。

Method: 使用NASA Ames火星全球气候模型，引入双峰沙尘粒径分布，耦合摩擦起电与静电诊断方案，将碰撞充电与大气局地动力学状态相关联。聚焦沙尘扬起与积累阶段，量化电场能量密度和放电特征（阈值、频率、空间聚集）。

Result: 发现广阔的风暴活跃电化带，南部中低纬度阈值超越最频繁，北部中低纬度次之。近地表电场达10²–10³ V/m，与小尺度沙尘现象相当。电场受沙尘载荷、湍流碰撞和电导率弛豫三者相互作用控制，昼夜电导率变化强烈抑制白天电场积累，多数事件处于弱辉光或Townsend放电 regime。

Conclusion: 模型捕获了电学有利条件的大尺度分布，但预测的活动空间范围可能是上限。结果为识别GDS期间静电放电最可能区域提供了定量框架，可为仪器设计、电源保护和任务规划提供指导。

Abstract: Planet-encircling dust storms fundamentally reshape Martian weather and the near-surface electrostatic environment. We investigate the generation and evolution of electric fields during global dust storms using bimodal dust size distributions from the NASA Ames Mars Global Climate Model, coupled with a triboelectric charging and electrostatic diagnostic scheme that links collisional charging to the local dynamical state of the atmosphere. Focusing on the dust-lifting and buildup phase and its subsequent evolution, we quantify the electric-field energy density and discharge characteristics, including onset thresholds, event frequency, and spatial clustering. The simulations reveal broad storm-active belts of enhanced electrification, with the most frequent threshold exceedances occurring in southern low-to-mid latitudes and secondary activity in northern low-to-mid latitudes. Modeled near-surface electric fields reach $10^{2}$--$10^{3}\ \mathrm{V\,m^{-1}}$ comparable to values inferred for smaller-scale dust phenomena. The results indicate that electric-field generation is controlled by the interplay between dust loading, turbulence-driven collisional activity, and conductivity-dependent charge relaxation, with diurnal conductivity variations strongly suppressing daytime electric-field buildup and most events remaining in the weak glow or Townsend discharge regime. While the model captures the large-scale distribution of electrically favorable conditions, the predicted spatial extent of activity likely represents an upper bound, as small-scale turbulent structures are not fully resolved. These results provide a quantitative framework to identify regions where electrostatic discharges are most likely during GDSs and to inform instrument design, power-system protection, and operations planning for future robotic and human missions.
