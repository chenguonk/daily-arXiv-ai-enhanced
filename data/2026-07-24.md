### [1] [Evidence for a giant companion orbiting Tabby's star](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.20643)
*Cristina Madurga-Favieres,P. A. Strøm,Thomas G. Wilson,Neda Heidari,Flavien Kiefer*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 对KIC 8462852（塔比星）的TESS和径向速度数据分析发现，其不规则的亮度变暗现象可能由一个轨道周期约1030天的巨行星/褐矮星伴星引起，该候选体的质量约为9.4倍木星质量，半径为1.7倍木星半径。彗星模型无法很好解释TESS观测到的对称凌星事件，支持了行星扰动彗星群的假说。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: KIC 8462852（塔比星）的开普勒光度曲线显示了深达20%的不规则亮度变暗现象，这些奇特事件没有周期性，其起源尚不明确。虽然主流解释认为尘埃来自系外彗星家族或星子碎片，但TESS数据发现了一个独特的对称凌星事件，可能对应行星或褐矮星。本研究旨在通过TESS数据、档案和新获取的径向速度数据，探测可能的行星伴星，并验证"行星轨道扰动引发彗星群"的理论。

Method: 分析TESS光度数据中发现的对称凌星事件；结合档案和新获取的径向速度数据搜索可能的轨道周期；利用开普勒巡天和其他测光调查数据约束可能的轨道周期窗口；对前两个轨道周期窗口进行后续观测，缩小允许范围；使用系外彗星模型对TESS凌星进行拟合，并与巨行星伴星假说比较；根据径向速度数据计算伴星的质量约束。

Result: 发现一个轨道周期至少约1030天的候选伴星；在开普勒等测光约束下，仅有五个可能的轨道周期窗口，后续观测进一步缩小了前两个窗口的范围；彗星模型拟合TESS凌星效果差或需要物理上不现实的参数，支持巨行星伴星假说；径向速度数据在2.3σ置信度下检测到质量为9.4^{+4.9}_{-4.1}倍木星质量（3σ上限低于28倍木星质量）的伴星；该伴星半径为1.7倍木星半径，处于巨行星-褐矮星过渡区域。

Conclusion: TESS观测到的对称凌星事件更可能由巨行星/褐矮星伴星而非彗星群引起；径向速度数据提供了该伴星的初步证据（2.3σ），但需要更多观测确认；该候选伴星处于巨行星和褐矮星之间的质量范围；未来的盖亚DR4天体测量数据将揭示该天体的真实性质。

Abstract: KIC 8462852, also known as Tabby's star, shows deep, irregular flux variations up to 20 per cent in its Kepler light curve. These unusual events show no apparent periodicity. Multi-wavelength observations show the dips are consistent with transiting dust, although its origin remains uncertain. The leading explanation is that dust may originate from a family of exocomets or planetesimal fragments. TESS data reveal a unique, symmetric transit event consistent with a planet or brown dwarf. This would support the theory that incoming exocometary bodies may result from orbital perturbations by a planet. The analysis of the TESS and, archival and newly obtained radial velocity (RV) data, suggests that a companion could orbit around Tabby's star with a period of at least ~1030 d. When combined with the constraints imposed by Kepler and other photometric surveys, we find only five orbital period windows are possible. We conduct observations to follow-up the first two windows, significantly reducing the allowed ranges. Fitting the TESS transit using exocometary models produces a poor fit or the need of physically unrealistic parameters, favouring the giant companion hypothesis. A 2.3 $σ$ detection of $M=9.4^{+4.9}_{-4.1}$ M$_{\mathrm{Jup}}$ (3$σ$ upper limit < 28.0 M$_{\mathrm{Jup}}$) at the transit orbital period provides tentative evidence of the companion, but more RV observations are required. Along with the radius $R=1.7\pm0.1$ R$_{\mathrm{Jup}}$, the companion would lie in the giant planet-brown dwarf regime. In the future Gaia DR4 astrometry will reveal the nature of this object.

### [2] [Magnetohydrodynamical opening of dust traps in protoplanetary disks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.21350)
*Sergey Khaibrakhmanov,Vitaly Akimkin*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本文研究发现，原行星盘中的大尺度磁场会导致气体旋转速度偏离开普勒速度1-2%，这种磁流体动力学效应会加速大尘埃颗粒向内漂移，抵消环状结构内部边缘的正压力梯度造成的向外漂移，从而破坏尘埃聚集和流不稳定性发生条件，对行星形成理论提出挑战。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统观点认为原行星盘中观测到的环状结构是局部压力极大值区域，能够高效聚集尘埃并促进行星形成。作者质疑这一范式，希望探讨大尺度磁场应力对气体旋转速度的影响是否会改变这一图景。

Method: 通过数值模拟，研究了典型湍流盘中大尺度磁场的动力学效应，分析磁场导致的开普勒旋转偏差对尘埃颗粒（粒径>1μm，斯托克斯数0.01-0.1）径向漂移速度的影响，并评估了不同湍流参数（α=10⁻³和10⁻²）下环状结构的尘埃捕获条件。

Result: 1) 磁场可导致盘外围气体旋转偏离开普勒速度1-2%；2) 大颗粒向内漂移速度可提升至原来的两倍；3) 这种MHD偏差不依赖于局部气体压力梯度，仅导致向恒星漂移；4) 在α=10⁻³的盘中，半宽10 au、密度对比度60%的环状结构会受到影响（α=10⁻²时需200%密度对比度）。

Conclusion: 原行星盘中大尺度磁场的存在要么完全阻止尘埃积累，要么对流不稳定性在密度环中的发生施加了更严格的条件，这对传统基于压力环的尘埃聚集和行星形成模型提出了重要修正。

Abstract: Observed ring-like structures in protoplanetary disks are often interpreted as local pressure maxima, which induce efficient dust concentration. We revisit this paradigm, considering the effect of the large-scale magnetic field stresses on the gas rotation speed. Our simulations show that the magnetic field can be dynamically strong and cause $1-2$% deviation from the Keplerian rotation at the periphery of a typical turbulent disk with dust grains of size $> 1\,μ$m. This effect increases the inward drift speed of large grains characterized by Stokes number of $0.01-0.1$ by up to two times in our simulations. Importantly, such MHD deviation from the Keplerian rotation does not depend on the local gas pressure gradient and leads to drift towards the star only. The fast drift induced by this effect can cancel out the outward drift caused by the positive pressure gradient at the inner edge of a ring and open up the dust trap. For the disks with turbulence parameter $α=10^{-3}$, this effect appears in the rings with a half-width of $10$ au and a density contrast up to $60$% ($200$% for $α=10^{-2}$). Thus, the presence of a large-scale magnetic field in protoplanetary disks either completely prevents or imposes stricter conditions for dust accumulation and the onset of the streaming instability in the density rings in protoplanetary disks.

### [3] [Experimental Measurements of Benzene Ice Critical Saturation Ratios on Titan Tholins and Impact on the Microphysical Modeling of Titan's South Polar Benzene Cloud](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.21415)
*David Dubois,Erika Barth,Laura T. Iraci,Ella Sciamma-O'Brien,Farid Salama,Sandrine Vinatier*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本文通过实验测量了苯（C6H6）冰在低温下在泰坦气溶胶类似物上的临界过饱和度，并将其纳入CARMA微物理模型，发现模拟的苯云形成高度比观测值低约20公里，推测可能是由于多物种共冷凝所致。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 卡西尼CIRS仪器在泰坦南极平流层观测到苯冰云，但其形成机制和微物理过程尚不清楚，需要实验数据和模型来理解气溶胶作为凝结核的作用。

Method: 1) 在NASA Ames COSmIC装置中产生泰坦气溶胶类似物（tholins）；2) 测量纯苯冰在低温（138-157 K）下的临界过饱和度Scrit；3) 对比裸基底与tholins基底上的沉积行为；4) 将Scrit数据转化为接触参数m，并纳入CARMA模型进行微物理模拟。

Result: 苯冰在tholins上的Scrit值范围为13.8（138 K）到3.3（157 K）；气溶胶显著促进苯冰成核；接触参数m = 2.6e-4 T + 0.9494；模拟云层高度比观测低约20公里。

Conclusion: 实验数据揭示了tholins作为苯冰凝结核的关键作用；模拟与观测的高度差异提示苯云可能受多物种共冷凝过程影响，而非纯苯单组分冷凝。

Abstract: The Cassini Composite Infrared Spectrometer (CIRS) revealed the presence of a benzene (C6H6) ice cloud in Titan's autumn south polar stratosphere, following the northern spring equinox in August 2009. This event increased the mixing ratio of benzene and raised the cloud top altitude. We present here new experimental measurements of the critical saturation (Scrit) of pure C6H6 ice at low temperature (from 13.8 at 138 K to 3.3 at 157 K), representative of Titan's atmospheric temperatures, when deposited onto Titan aerosol analogs (tholins) produced in the NASA Ames COsmic SImulation Chamber (COSmIC). Comparisons of Scrit values of benzene vapor deposition obtained on blank substrates versus on Titan tholins reveals the highly favorable role aerosols play as condensation nuclei. We have included these new Scrit measurements to calculate the nucleation contact parameter from m = 2.6e-4 T + 0.9494 in the Community Aerosol and Radiation Model for Atmospheres (CARMA) to investigate the variation in size and number density of C6H6 cloud particles as a function of altitude in Titan's southern polar atmosphere at 87 S. We discuss how these new temperature-dependent measurements impact the microphysical modeling and result in simulated benzene clouds forming about 20 km lower than seen in previous observations. A possible explanation for this discrepancy is that the cloud system was likely influenced by the co-condensation of more than one volatile gas species.
