### [1] [TOI-3664 b, TOI-4034 b & TOI-6564 b: Three new hot Jupiters around stars approaching the terminal age main sequence](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05295)

TL;DR: 本文通过TESS发现并确认了三颗围绕主序星晚期恒星运行的热木星(TOI-3664 b, TOI-4034 b, TOI-6564 b)，它们具有亚木星质量但半径大于木星的特点，为研究热木星后期演化提供了新的样本。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究热木星演化需要涵盖不同演化阶段的特征化系统样本，而主序星晚期这一阶段的样本相对匮乏。

Method: 利用TESS数据发现候选体，通过CARMENES、CORALIE和MINERVA-Australis等光谱巡天项目验证确认；结合天体测量、陀螺年代学、恒星等时线和锂丰度等多种方法精确测定系统年龄。

Abstract: Studying the evolution of hot Jupiters requires a sample of well-characterised systems across all evolutionary states. We present three new gas giant exoplanets around stars approaching the end of the main sequence, a comparatively unexplored epoch of hot Jupiter evolution. These planets were discovered by TESS before being vetted and confirmed through dedicated spectroscopic follow-up programmes by CARMENES, CORALIE and MINERVA-Australis. TOI-3664 b has a period of 3.30 days, a radius of 1.22 +/- 0.03 RJup and a mass of 0.36 +/- 0.12 MJup. TOI-4034 b is a short-period hot Jupiter with a period of 1.80 days, a radius of 1.58 +/- 0.02 RJup and a mass of 0.87 +/- 0.16 MJup. Meanwhile TOI-6564 b has a period of 3.99 days, radius of 1.46 +/- 0.02 RJup and mass of 0.70 +/- 0.07 MJup. All three planets have radii larger than Jupiter but sub-Jupiter masses, in line with slight inflation as their hosts increase in luminosity towards the end of the main sequence. These exoplanets' low densities and hosts' advanced evolutionary states make them interesting planets with which to study the later stages of hot Jupiter evolution. Careful analysis was undertaken to determine the ages of each system, considering astrometry, gyrochronology, stellar isochrones and lithium abundance, yielding ages of 9.0 +2.4/-2.1 Gyr, 5.7 +/- 0.5 Gyr and 4.0 +/- 1.0 Gyr for TOI-3664, TOI-4034 and TOI-6564 respectively, yet each system has a similar evolutionary state because of their differing stellar masses (0.98 +/- 0.03, 1.19 +0.13/-0.03 and 1.18 +0.16/-0.03 M*). These three planets add more steps to the "age-ladder" of exoplanetary evolution, building towards the community's goal of understanding how planets evolve over time.

### [2] [Sky-Plane Velocity Distributions of Interstellar Objects and Implications for Their Detection](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05344)
*Cassidy E. Walker,Darryl Z. Seligman*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本文研究了星际天体因高速天球运动而难以被发现的概率，给出了解析解，并通过合成种群模拟发现，暗弱的天体更容易被遗漏，暗示太阳系中存在更多未被探测到的星际天体。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 过去十年已发现三个宏观尺度的星际天体，暗示银河系中存在大量此类天体。然而，这些天体可能因快速的天球运动速度而难以被发现，因此需要定量评估其可见性限制。

Method: 推导了任意轨道上物体在任意观测位置的天球运动解析解；生成三组约10^5个星际天体的合成种群（半径1.2、3.0、5.0 AU），计算它们在多种极限星等下的天球运动速度；对比三类已知星际天体的特征。

Result: 1）已知的三个星际天体的天球运动速度与具有相似绝对星等的种群特征一致；2）绝对亮度更高的天体在更低的运动速度下达到探测阈值，而暗弱天体速度更高；3）活跃彗星的探测速度更低；4）速度分布尾部超过1I的发现速度。

Conclusion: 快速运动的星际天体（尤其是暗弱天体）难以被追踪关联，这意味着可能有更多未被探测到的星际天体正在穿越太阳系。

Abstract: In the past decade, three macroscopic-scale interstellar objects have been discovered, implying that a larger galactic population exists. In this paper, we investigate the possibility that the rapid sky-plane velocities of interstellar objects may preclude their discovery. We provide an analytic solution for the apparent sky motion of an object on an arbitrary orbit observed at an arbitrary location which (i) is more efficient and (ii) requires less overhead than the numerical approach. This formula is applied to evaluate the typical sky motion of an interstellar object as a function of its orbit and limiting magnitude/distance. We generate three synthetic populations of $\sim10^5$ interstellar objects within heliocentric spheres of radii 1.2, 3.0, and 5.0 AU, and calculate the sky motion for these objects when they reach a range of limiting magnitudes for multiple populations of interstellar asteroids and comets. The sky motions of the three known interstellar objects are broadly characteristic of populations with similar absolute magnitudes. Moreover, the intrinsically brighter objects reach detection magnitude thresholds at lower speeds than the dim objects, and active comets at even lower speeds for the same apparent magnitudes. The tails of these distributions extend to speeds faster than the discovery motion of 1I. Therefore, the difficulties associated with linking rapidly moving interstellar objects, especially those with intrinsically dim properties, could imply that more exist undetected traversing the Solar System.

### [3] [Ultraviolet-Driven Atmospheric Degeneracies Challenge Conventional Biosignature Frameworks for Terrestrial Planets with Ultracool M Dwarf Hosts: An Archean-Analog TRAPPIST-1 e Case Study](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05451)
*Evan L. Sneed,Edward W. Schwieterman,Sarah R. Peacock,Nicholas F. Wogan,Timothy W. Lyons*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: TRAPPIST-1的紫外线光谱不确定性会剧烈改变其行星（如TRAPPIST-1 e）的大气化学成分，导致CH₄、CO、O₂和O₃等生物标志物浓度产生数量级差异，甚至出现假阳性生物信号，需降低UV不确定性才能准确解读宜居行星大气。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: TRAPPIST-1作为M8V超冷矮星，其紫外光谱能量分布存在显著争议，而这种不确定性会直接影响行星大气化学组成和生物信号解读，因此需要量化评估UV SED差异对类太古地球大气模型的影响。

Method: 使用一维光化学模型，在TRAPPIST-1 e上模拟太古地球大气类比，分别考虑有/无生物圈的情况，输入不同的UV SED数据，对比分析大气成分变化。

Result: 不同恒星光谱导致CH₄、CO、O₂和O₃丰度产生数量级差异；一种UV重建下，非生物条件可同时产生CH₄和O₃的假阳性不平衡生物信号；所有SED中，微生物介导的CO消耗可在无产氧光合作用下积累大量O₂和O₃；CO是区分生物与非生物边界条件的关键示踪物。

Conclusion: 紫外线光谱不确定性是解读超冷矮星宜居行星大气生物信号的主要障碍，降低UV观测误差对未来寻找生命至关重要。

Abstract: The ultraviolet (UV) spectrum of a host star strongly shapes the atmospheric composition and potential biosignatures of its planets. This relationship may be especially important for the planets orbiting TRAPPIST-1, an M8V star with substantially different published UV spectral energy distributions (SEDs). Using a one-dimensional photochemical model, we quantify how these SED uncertainties affect Archean Earth-like atmospheric analogs on TRAPPIST-1 e with and without biospheres. We emphasize Earth's Archean epoch because it represents a planet in transition from primarily abiotic to biotic controls on atmospheric composition. Different stellar spectra produce order-of-magnitude variations in the predicted abundances of CH4, CO, O2, and O3, thereby generating photochemical degeneracies that complicate the interpretation of potential biosignatures. For one TRAPPIST-1 UV reconstruction, a modeled atmosphere with abiotic deposition velocities and volcanic CH4 input can sustain simultaneous spectrally discernible CH4 and O3, yielding a potential false-positive disequilibrium biosignature. For all SEDs tested, surface deposition consistent with microbially-mediated CO consumption allows substantial O2 and O3 accumulation even without oxygenic photosynthesis, implying that oxygen-rich atmospheres around ultracool M dwarfs may not uniquely trace oxygenic ecosystems. Across our models, CO remains a powerful discriminator between abiotic and biotic surface boundary assumptions. Overall, we show that the abundances of co-occurring CH4, CO, and O3 can vary by orders of magnitude, depending on the assumed UV SED, creating ambiguities in interpreting atmospheric biosignatures, though observability may be challenging with current capabilities. Reducing UV spectral uncertainties is therefore essential for assessing surface-to-atmosphere interactions of temperate exoplanets around ultracool M dwarfs.

C-type and dark X-type asteroids typically exhibit flat to slightly negative and positive spectral slopes, respectively. ### [5] [Morphological and mineralogical characterization of the Freundlich-Sharonov Basin: Implications for the lunar farside magmatism](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06023)

### [6] [Peas and USPs: Can Stellar Spindown and Peas in a Pod Replicate Ultra-Short-Period Planet Characteristics?](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06230)
*Adam Distler,Juliette Becker*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本文通过拉普拉斯-拉格朗日长期摄动理论，研究了恒星自转减慢如何使PIAP系统（行星间距规则、大小相似的系统）解耦。发现严格PIAP系统在恒星J₂演化下无法经历长期共振穿越，需要内行星向内迁移才能实现共振穿越。利用恒星演化模型预测的J₂演化轨迹，展示了共振穿越时间的多样性，可用于检验行星迁移路径和初始恒星倾角。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: PIAP系统在系外行星系统中很常见，但超短周期行星与PIAP系统存在明显差异（更高的相互倾角、年龄和行星大小）。本文旨在探究恒星自转减慢能否使PIAP系统解耦，从而解释这些差异的起源。

Method: 使用拉普拉斯-拉格朗日长期摄动理论，分析恒星J₂演化对PIAP系统的影响；结合恒星演化模型得到的J₂演化轨迹，模拟共振穿越时间。

Conclusion: 该框架可用于检验不同的行星迁移路径和初始恒星倾角，为理解PIAP系统与超短周期行星之间的差异提供理论依据。

Abstract: Peas-in-a-Pod (PIAP) systems have been shown to be common across exoplanet systems, with regular planet spacings and similar planet sizes. In contrast, ultra-short-period planets have displayed distinct differences from PIAP systems, including higher mutual inclinations, ages, and planet sizes. Using Laplace-Lagrange secular theory, we investigate the ability of stellar spindown to decouple PIAP systems. We find that strictly PIAP systems with regular spacings cannot undergo secular resonance crossings for the expected stellar $J_2$ evolution, and that we instead require the inner planet to migrate inward to undergo this resonance crossing. As a result, there is no inner edge to PIAP systems where systems will always cross a secular resonance and decouple the inner planet. Using expected $J_2$ evolution tracks from stellar evolution models, we find a diversity of expected resonance crossing times, highlighting the ability to test migration pathways and initial stellar obliquities using this framework.
