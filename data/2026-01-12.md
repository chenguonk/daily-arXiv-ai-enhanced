### [1] [Detection of the 2021 Arid Meteor Shower on Maunakea, Hawai'i](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.05412)
*Ichi Tanaka,Hitoshi Hasegawa,Toyokazu Uda,Mikiya Sato,Jun-ichi Watanabe,Masanobu Higashiyama*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 首次成功探测到预测的"Arid"流星雨，使用安装在夏威夷Maunakea的Subaru望远镜圆顶上的YouTube直播相机，通过公民科学视觉检查和自动检测验证确认了流星雨活动。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 预测的"Arid"流星雨（IAU#1130 ARD）将在2021年首次出现，需要观测验证。北半球此时的光学观测极为有限，因此这次观测具有独特价值。

Method: 使用安装在Subaru望远镜圆顶上的公开YouTube直播相机，具有宽视场（70°×40°）和高灵敏度。通过两种方式检测流星：公民科学家的视觉检查和后续的自动检测验证。

Result: 确认从预测辐射点附近出现的流星数量比前后几天增加了6倍以上（~9σ）。观测时间在预测峰值后4-5小时，显示活动尚未减弱。流星特征为缓慢而暗淡，但也观测到几个较亮的带尾迹流星。

Conclusion: 模拟显示这次探测可由母体彗星15P/Finlay在2008年或2014年释放的尘埃轨迹解释，但2008年彗星活动异常平静，而2014年有多次大爆发。因此，这次在夏威夷探测到的流星雨尘埃源很可能来自2014年爆发期间高速（~67 m/s）喷射的尘埃。

Abstract: We report the successful detection of the "Arid" Meteor Shower (IAU\#1130 ARD), predicted to emerge for the first time in 2021, using a publicly accessible YouTube live camera developed by us. This live camera, installed on the Subaru Telescope dome in the summit region of Maunakea, Hawai'i, features a wide field of view (70 deg by 40 deg) and high sensitivity, capable of observing stars fainter than 6th magnitude. Meteor detection was performed in two ways: visual inspection by citizen viewers and subsequent validation through automated detection. As a result, we confirmed that the number of meteors appearing from near the predicted radiant increased by more than six times (~9 sigma) compared to the preceding and following days. Our observation time was 4-5 hours after the predicted peak (solar longitude = 193.9 deg), providing clear data indicating that the activity had not yet declined. Optical observations at this time from the Northern Hemisphere are extremely limited and unique, making our observation point valuable. The meteors are characterized as slow and faint appearance, but several brighter meteors with wakes were also observed. Simulations tracing the dust trails from the parent body, Comet 15P/Finlay, suggest that our detection can be explained by either the dust trails released in 2008 or 2014, both requiring high ejection velocities. However, during the comet's 2008 return, its activity was exceptionally quiet, making a high-velocity dust ejection questionable. On the other hand, multiple large outbursts were observed during the 2014 return, at which time a certain amount of high-velocity dust release is expected. We conclude that the dust source of the meteor shower detected in Hawai'i this time is likely attributable to high-velocity (~67 m s-1) dust ejected during the 2014 outburst.

### [2] [Stellar control on atmospheric carbon chemistry, CO runaway, and organic synthesis on lifeless Earth-like planets](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.05480)
*Yoshiaki Endo,Yasuto Watanabe,Kazumi Ozaki*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 研究開發整合模型探討類地行星大氣碳物種（CO2、CO、CH4）隨軌道距離的變化規律，發現外宜居帶邊緣可能發生CO失控現象，而甲醛生成則在更熱恆星附近最大化。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 大氣碳物種（CO2、CO、CH4）對類地行星的氣候、氧化還原狀態和生命前環境具有根本性控制作用。隨著系外行星大氣表徵技術的進步，需要理解這些物種在不同恆星和行星條件下如何被調控。

Method: 開發整合數值模型，耦合大氣化學、氣候和長期碳循環，研究繞太陽類恆星（F、G、K型）運行的無生命類地行星的大氣組成。

Result: CO2、CO和CH4通常隨軌道距離增加而增加；外宜居帶邊緣行星可能發生CO失控（由OH自由基嚴重消耗驅動的光化學不穩定性）。CO失控閾值強烈依賴恆星光譜類型，在較冷、質量較小的恆星周圍最易觸發。甲醛生成在繞質量更大、紫外輻射更強的恆星運行的行星上達到峰值，且軌道距離剛好在CO失控閾值內側時最大化。

Conclusion: 建立了一個定量框架，將可觀測系統特性（恆星類型和軌道距離）與無生命類地行星的大氣碳化學聯繫起來，為解釋未來光譜觀測和評估此類行星維持生命前化學的潛力提供了新背景。

Abstract: The abundances of atmospheric carbon species--carbon dioxide (CO2), carbon monoxide (CO), and methane (CH4)--exert fundamental controls on the climate, redox state, and prebiotic environment of terrestrial planets. As exoplanet atmospheric characterization advances, it is essential to understand how these species are regulated on habitable terrestrial planets across a wide range of stellar and planetary conditions. Here, we develop an integrated numerical model that couples atmospheric chemistry, climate, and the long-term carbon cycle to investigate the atmospheric compositions of lifeless, Earth-like planets orbiting Sun-like (F-, G-, and K-type) stars. Our simulations demonstrate that CO2, CO, and CH4 generally increase with orbital distance, and that planets near the outer edge of the habitable zone may undergo CO runaway--a photochemical instability driven by severe depletion of OH radicals. The threshold for CO runaway depends strongly on stellar spectral type and is most easily triggered around cooler, lower-mass stars. In contrast, the atmospheric production of formaldehyde (H2CO)--a key precursor for prebiotic organic chemistry--peaks around planets orbiting more massive, UV-luminous stars and is maximized at orbital distances just interior to the CO-runaway threshold. These results establish a quantitative framework linking observable system properties--stellar type and orbital distance--and the atmospheric carbon chemistry of lifeless Earth-like planets, providing new context for interpreting future spectroscopic observations and for evaluating the potential of such planets to sustain prebiotic chemistry.

### [4] [On the orbital evolution of binaries with polar circumbinary disks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.05932)
*Cheng Chen,Philip J. Armitage,C. J. Nixon*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 研究使用流体动力学模拟发现，偏心双星周围的极向排列盘会导致双星轨道收缩，且收缩速率比对齐或逆行盘更快，这对行星形成和超大质量黑洞合并有重要意义。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 许多天体物理系统中的双星周围存在盘状结构，当盘与双星轨道平面不共面时，特别是对于偏心双星，盘可能演化成极向排列（盘角动量矢量与双星偏心率矢量对齐）。这种配置对双星轨道演化的影响尚不清楚，对行星形成和超大质量黑洞合并有重要意义。

Method: 使用平滑粒子流体动力学（SPH）模拟，在质量注入条件下演化到近似稳态，研究极向排列盘对双星轨道演化的影响。模拟了不同双星-盘参数，并考虑了汇半径大小的影响。

Result: 在所有模拟案例中，双星轨道都随时间收缩。收缩速率比具有相同盘参数的对齐或逆行盘情况更大。收缩速率基本不受双星汇半径大小影响，但对于足够小的汇半径，部分模型出现长期存在的极向环绕主星盘，不断从环双星盘获得质量。

Conclusion: 极向排列盘会加速双星轨道收缩，这对年轻极向排列盘中的行星形成和星系中心超大质量黑洞合并有重要影响。研究结果为理解这些天体物理过程提供了新见解。

Abstract: Binaries occur in many astrophysical systems, from young protostellar binaries in star forming regions to supermassive black hole binaries in galaxy centers. In many cases, a circumbinary disk of gas forms around the binary with an orbit that may be misaligned to the binary plane. Misaligned disks around nearly circular binaries evolve into disks that are either aligned or counteraligned with the binary orbit. However, if the binary is sufficiently eccentric, then it can be more likely that the disk ends up in a polar-aligned configuration in which the disk angular momentum vector aligns with the binary eccentricity vector. We use Smoothed Particle Hydrodynamics simulations, evolved to an approximate steady state under mass injection, to determine the orbital evolution of a binary with a polar-aligned disk for a range of binary-disk parameters. We find that, in all of the cases we have simulated, the binary shrinks with time. The decay rate is larger than for binaries surrounded by aligned or retrograde disks with matched disk parameters. The rate of shrinkage is largely unaltered by the size of the sink radii employed for the binary stars, but for small enough sink radii some of the models exhibit long-lived polar circumprimary disks, which are continually fed mass from the circumbinary disk. We discuss our results in the contexts of planet formation in young polar-aligned disks and merging supermassive black holes in galaxy centers.
