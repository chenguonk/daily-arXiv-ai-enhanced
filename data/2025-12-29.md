### [1] [Elemental abundance pattern and temperature inversion on the dayside of HAT-P-70b observed with CARMENES and PEPSI](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.21470)
*B. Guo,F. Yan,Th. Henning,L. Nortmann,M. Stangret,D. Cont,E. Pallé,D. Shulyak,K. G. Strassmeier,I. Ilyin,F. Lesjak,A. Reiners,S. Liu,K. Molaverdikhani,G. Scandariato,E. Keles,J. A. Caballero,P. J. Amado,A. Quirrenbach,I. Ribas,S. Góngora,A. P. Hatzes,M. López-Puertas,D. Montes,K. Poppenhaeger,E. Schlawin,A. Schweitzer,D. Sicilia*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 首次在系外行星大气中检测到Al I和AlH，通过高分辨率光谱观测超热木星HAT-P-70b，发现了多种金属元素和离子的发射信号，揭示了大气热反转和可能的流体动力学逃逸过程。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 通过观测超热木星HAT-P-70b的大气化学成分，研究行星形成过程中难熔元素的丰度，探索大气热结构和化学平衡状态，为理解行星形成和大气演化提供关键信息。

Method: 使用CARMENES和PEPSI高分辨率光谱仪观测HAT-P-70b的昼侧热发射光谱，通过交叉相关分析检测大气化学成分，并进行大气反演以约束热剖面和元素丰度。

Result: 检测到Al I、AlH、Ca II、Cr I、Fe I、Fe II、Mg I、Mn I、Ti I的发射信号（其中Al I和AlH为首次在系外行星中检测），并发现可能的C I、Ca I、Na I、NaH、Ni I信号。反演显示强热反转结构，金属丰度接近太阳值，但Ni可能富集，而Ca、Ti、V等元素略有贫化。金属离子（Fe II和Ca II）的体积混合比极高，与化学平衡模型预测不符。

Conclusion: HAT-P-70b大气存在强热反转，金属丰度总体接近太阳值但存在元素特异性差异。极高的金属离子丰度表明大气可能经历显著的流体动力学逃逸过程，导致高海拔区域离子线形成增强。Al的太阳丰度可能反映形成过程中的富集，而高凝结温度元素的贫化可能与夜侧冷阱有关。

Abstract: Ground-based high-resolution spectroscopy has identified various chemical species in the atmospheres of ultra-hot Jupiters, including neutral and ionized metals, providing key insights into planet formation through refractory element abundances. We observed the dayside thermal emission spectrum of the UHJ HAT-P-70b using the high-resolution spectrographs CARMENES and PEPSI. Through cross-correlation analysis, we detect emission signals of Al i, AlH, Ca ii, Cr i, Fe i, Fe ii, Mg i, Mn i, and Ti i, marking the first detection of Al i and AlH in an exoplanetary atmosphere. Tentative signals of C i, Ca i, Na i, NaH, and Ni i are also identified. These detections enable atmospheric retrievals to constrain the thermal profile and elemental abundances of the planet's dayside hemisphere. The retrieved temperature-pressure profile reveals a strong thermal inversion. The chemical free retrieval yields a metallicity of [Fe/H] = 0.38(+0.74/-1.11), while the chemical equilibrium retrieval gives [Fe/H] = 0.23(+1.08/-0.98), both consistent with solar metallicity. We also tentatively find an enhanced abundance of Ni, possibly due to the accretion of Ni-rich planetesimals during formation. On the other hand, elements with condensation temperatures above 1400 K (e.g., Ca, Ti, and V) appear slightly depleted, which may be caused by nightside cold trapping. However, Al, with the highest condensation temperature at 1653K, displays a solar like abundance, which might reflect the formation-related enrichment of Al. Our retrieval indicates extremely high volume mixing ratios of metal ions (Fe ii and Ca ii), which are significantly inconsistent with predictions from chemical equilibrium models. This disequilibrium suggests that the atmosphere is likely undergoing significant hydrodynamic escaping, which enhances the atmospheric density at high altitudes where the ionic lines are formed.

### [2] [Episodic planetesimal disruptions triggered by dissipation of gas disk](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.21493)
*Kang Shuai,Li-Yong Zhou,Hejiu Hui*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 行星微星在高速碰撞中会发生灾难性破坏。陨石记录显示太阳系早期频繁的灾难性碰撞，但星云气体会抑制碰撞频率。本文发现木星和土星的长期共振与木星的平均运动共振共同作用，能在星云消散期间触发大量灾难性碰撞。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 陨石中的放射性同位素测年证实了太阳系早期频繁的灾难性碰撞，反映了当时的暴力环境。然而，星云气体可以抑制行星微星的偏心率，从而减少碰撞频率。因此需要寻找能够激发原行星盘的强动力学机制。

Method: 研究木星和土星在星云气体消散过程中产生的长期共振扫掠效应，结合木星的平均运动共振，分析这些共振如何触发行星微星的灾难性碰撞。特别关注当长期共振位于约2-3天文单位时的碰撞事件。

Result: 长期共振和平均运动共振共同作用能够在星云消散期间触发大量灾难性碰撞，这些碰撞在长期共振位于约2-3天文单位时呈爆发性发生，并在之后持续。气体消散完成后，灾难性碰撞频率下降，行星胚胎的散射成为碰撞的主要驱动力。

Conclusion: 由长期共振和平均运动共振激发的暴力环境在星云消散期间的原行星盘中可能普遍存在，这解释了太阳系早期频繁的灾难性碰撞事件，为理解行星形成过程中的动力学环境提供了重要机制。

Abstract: Catastrophic disruptions of planetesimals occur in high-velocity collisions. Radioisotope dating of planetesimal disruption events recorded in meteorites confirms frequent catastrophic collisions in the first $\sim$10 Myr of the Solar System, reflecting a violent environment of the time. However, the nebula gas can damp the eccentricity of planetesimals and suppress the frequency of planetesimal collisions. Strong dynamical mechanisms that excited the protoplanetary disk are required. Here we show that the sweeping secular resonances of Jupiter and Saturn induced by the nebular gas dissipation, together with the mean motion resonances of Jupiter, can trigger a large number of catastrophic collisions, which occur episodically when the secular resonances are at $\sim$2-3 au and continue thereafter. After the gas dissipation completes, catastrophic collisions decrease in frequency, with scattering by planetary embryos becoming the major driving force of the collisions. Our results suggest that the violent environment excited by secular and mean motion resonances can be ubiquitous in protoplanetary disks during nebula dissipation.

### [3] [Numerical simulation of lunar response to gravitational waves and its 3D topographic effect using the spectral-element method](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.21667)
*Lei Zhang,Han Yan,Jinhai Zhang,Xian Chen*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 开发了三维谱元法模拟月球对低频引力波的响应，验证了方法精度，发现月球地形对引力波放大有不可忽视的影响


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 月球作为天然的韦伯棒可以放大引力波信号，但缺乏三维数值模拟方法来准确确定放大效应

Method: 开发了高阶三维有限元方法（谱元法，SEM）来数值模拟月球对20 mHz以下引力波的响应

Result: 方法验证显示频率偏差小于3%（首个峰值约1 mHz）和小于0.8%（后续峰值至10 mHz）；三维地形效应导致引力波信号在特定频率成分增强

Conclusion: 月球表面地形对引力波响应有不可忽视的影响，为全面评估月球共振响应和规划月球引力波探测提供了基础

Abstract: The Moon has been regarded as a natural Weber bar capable of amplifying gravitational waves (GWs) for detecting events across a wide range of frequencies. However, accurately determining the amplification effects remains challenging due to the absence of 3D numerical simulation methods. In this study, we develop a high-order 3D finite element method (spectral-element method, SEM) to numerically simulate the lunar response to GWs below 20 mHz. We verify the accuracy of our method by comparing the resonant peaks of our results with those from semi-analytical solutions and find that the frequency deviation is less than 3% for the first peak at about 1 mHz and less than 0.8% for the subsequent peaks up to 10 mHz. Using this method, we evaluate the amplification of GW signals due to 3D topographic effects of the Moon, and we find enhancements at a series of specific frequency components. These results highlight the non-negligible effect of surface topography on the lunar response to GWs, as a fundamental factor that holds significant implications across both global and regional analyses. Our work paves the way for a comprehensive evaluation of the Moon's resonant response to GWs, helpful for the strategic planning of lunar GW detections.

### [4] [From emission to absorption: the FAST observation of the OH 18-cm lines from the Comet C2025/A6](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.21969)
*Dongyue Jiang,Lei Qian,Minglei Guo,Qiaoli Hao,Menglin Huang,Peng Jiang,Hongfei Liu,Chun Sun,Xingyi Wang,Qingliang Yang,Naiping Yu,Lei Zhao,Yutao Zhao,Liyun Zhang,Yichi Zhang,Tongjie Zhang,Zhichen Pan*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 首次使用FAST望远镜超宽带接收器观测彗星C/2025 A6，检测到OH 18厘米谱线，发现谱线从发射到吸收的反转现象，并推导出水的膨胀速度和OH产率变化趋势。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 利用FAST望远镜的超宽带接收器首次观测彗星C/2025 A6的OH 18厘米谱线，研究彗星活动特性及其随日心距离的变化规律。

Method: 使用FAST望远镜超宽带接收器在2025年10月23日至11月8日期间观测彗星C/2025 A6，通过梯形拟合OH谱线轮廓，分析谱线反转现象，推导水的膨胀速度，并估算OH产率。

Result: 首次检测到彗星C/2025 A6的OH 18厘米谱线；观察到OH谱线从发射到吸收的反转现象，主要由日心速度变化引起；水的膨胀速度随日心距离减小而增加；OH产率呈现显著上升趋势。

Conclusion: FAST望远镜成功观测到彗星C/2025 A6的OH 18厘米谱线，揭示了谱线反转现象和水的膨胀速度变化规律，为理解彗星活动特性提供了重要观测数据。

Abstract: We observed comet C/2025 A6 with FAST telescope equipped with the ultra-wideband receiver from 23$^{\rm rd}$ October to 8$^{\rm th}$ November 2025 and detected the OH 18-cm lines for the first time. The OH lines underwent a reversal from emission to absorption from 23$^{\rm rd}$ October to 5$^{\rm th}$ November, which is mainly caused by variations in the heliocentric velocity. Through trapezoidal fitting of the OH line profiles, we derive expansion velocities of the water that rise as the heliocentric distance decreases. Based on these results, we estimated the OH production rates of C/2025 A6 for 23$^{\rm rd}$ October, 26$^{\rm th}$ October, 4$^{\rm th}$ November, and 5$^{\rm th}$ November and it presents a significant upward trend.
