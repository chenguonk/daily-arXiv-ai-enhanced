### [1] [Self-consistent Dynamical and Chaotic Tides in the REBOUNDx framework](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.04315)
*Donald J. Liveoak,Sarah C. Millholland,Michelle Vick,Daniel Tamayo*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 在REBOUNDx框架中实现动力学潮汐与轨道耦合演化，用于高偏心率天体系统研究


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 高偏心率轨道中，潮汐力会激发天体内部的振动模式（动力学潮汐），需要开发工具来研究这些效应

Method: 在REBOUNDx（REBOUND N体积分器的扩展）中实现动力学潮汐模式与轨道运动的耦合演化

Abstract: At high eccentricities, tidal forcing excites vibrational modes within orbiting bodies known as dynamical tides. In this paper, we implement the coupled evolution of these modes with the body's orbit in the \texttt{REBOUNDx} framework, an extension to the popular $N$-body integrator \texttt{REBOUND}. We provide a variety of test cases relevant to exoplanet dynamics and demonstrate overall agreement with prior studies of dynamical tides in the secular regime. Our implementation is readily applied to various high-eccentricity scenarios and allows for fast and accurate $N$-body investigations of astrophysical systems for which dynamical tides are relevant.

### [2] [Transit Photometry and Ephemeris Refinement of WASP-12 b Using TESS Data](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.04484)
*Chinedu Jude Nnaji*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 利用TESS数据对超热木星WASP-12 b进行详细的凌星测光分析，测量了行星参数并精化了轨道星历表，确认了该行星的高度膨胀特性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 利用TESS的高精度测光数据，对已知的超热木星WASP-12 b进行更精确的凌星参数测量和轨道星历表精化，为未来的大气和动力学研究提供更新的参数基础。

Method: 使用TESS的公开校准光变曲线和目标像素文件，通过MAST云基础设施获取数据。提取并归一化测光时间序列，使用初始星历表进行相位折叠，用物理凌星模型拟合以确定系统几何参数。测量单个凌星中时，通过加权线性拟合精化轨道星历表，并进行O-C分析检验时序变化。

Result: 测量了行星-恒星半径比、轨道倾角、撞击参数和凌星持续时间等几何参数，结合文献中的恒星参数推导出行星半径和凌星深度，确认了WASP-12 b的高度膨胀特性。精化了轨道周期和参考历元，提高了未来凌星时间的预测精度。O-C分析显示没有统计显著的凌星时序变化，数据在测量不确定度内与线性星历表一致。

Conclusion: 这项工作展示了TESS测光数据在精确表征凌星参数和精化星历表方面的能力，为WASP-12 b的未来大气和动力学研究提供了更新的参数，同时确认了该行星系统在TESS观测基线内具有稳定的线性轨道。

Abstract: We present a detailed transit photometric analysis of the ultra-hot Jupiter WASP-12 b using data from the Transiting Exoplanet Survey Satellite (TESS). The study is based on publicly available calibrated light curves and target pixel files accessed through the Mikulski Archive for Space Telescopes (MAST) cloud infrastructure. After extracting and normalizing the photometric time series, the light curve was phase-folded using an initial ephemeris and modeled with a physical transit model to determine the system's geometric parameters. From the transit modeling, we measure the planet-to-star radius ratio, orbital inclination, impact parameter, and transit duration. Adopting stellar parameters from the literature, we derive the planetary radius and transit depth, confirming the highly inflated nature of WASP-12 b. Individual mid-transit times were measured and used to refine the orbital ephemeris through a weighted linear fit. The resulting refined orbital period and reference epoch improve the predictive accuracy of future transit times over the TESS observational baseline. An observed-minus-calculated (O-C) analysis reveals no statistically significant transit timing variations, indicating that the timing data are consistent with a linear ephemeris within the measurement uncertainties. This work demonstrates the capability of TESS photometry to provide precise transit characterization and ephemeris refinement for well-studied exoplanet systems, and provides updated parameters that are relevant for future atmospheric and dynamical investigations of WASP-12 b.

### [3] [Investigating the High-energy Radiation Environment of Planets in Sun-like Binary Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.04593)
*Patrick R. Behr,Kevin France,Nicholas Kruczek,Nicholas Nell,Brian Fleming,Stefan Ulrich,Girish M. Duvvuri,Amy Louca,Yamila Miguel*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 利用SISTINE火箭光谱仪观测α Centauri AB双星系统，构建了从X射线到远红外的完整光谱能量分布，评估了双星环境下系外行星大气光化学和物质损失的影响。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 远紫外辐射是行星大气光化学的重要驱动源，准确理解行星所暴露的辐射环境对于解释大气观测数据至关重要。α Centauri作为距离最近的类太阳双星系统，是研究系外行星大气辐射环境的理想目标。

Method: 使用SISTINE火箭光谱仪同时观测α Centauri A和B的980-1570 Å远紫外光谱，结合历史观测数据、模型光谱和新的恒星活动模型，构建了5 Å-1 mm的完整光谱能量分布。利用VULCAN光化学动力学代码模拟最小和最大辐射通量下的大气化学成分变化。

Result: 获得了α Centauri双星系统最完整的远紫外光谱覆盖，发现双星轨道运动和恒星活动水平变化导致入射辐射通量随时间变化。模拟显示，对于围绕类太阳恒星的类地行星，双星环境增强的大气物质损失可能不会成为未来系外行星探测任务（如Habitable Worlds Observatory）的主要问题。

Conclusion: 双星系统中恒星伴星的轨道运动和活动水平变化会影响行星接收的辐射通量，但研究结果表明这种增强的大气损失效应对于在类太阳恒星周围寻找类地行星的探测任务可能不构成严重障碍。

Abstract: Far-ultraviolet (FUV) radiation is a driving source of photochemistry in planetary atmospheres. Proper interpretation of atmospheric observations requires a full understanding of the radiation environment that a planet is exposed to. Using the Suborbital Imaging Spectrograph for Transition-region Irradiance from Nearby Exoplanet host stars (SISTINE) rocket-borne spectrograph, we observed the Sun-like binary system $α$ Centauri AB and captured the FUV spectrum of both stars simultaneously. Our spectra cover 980--1570 Å, providing the broadest FUV wavelength coverage taken in a single exposure and spanning several key stellar emission features which are important photochemical drivers. Combining the SISTINE spectrum with archival observations, model spectra, and a novel stellar activity model, we have created spectral energy distributions (SEDs) spanning 5 Å--1 mm for both $α$ Centauri A and B. We use the SEDs to estimate the total high-energy flux (X-ray--UV) incident on a hypothetical exoplanet orbiting $α$ Centauri A. Because the incident flux varies over time due to the orbit of the stellar companion and the activity level of each star, we use the VULCAN photochemical kinetics code to estimate atmospheric chemical abundances in the case of minimum and maximum flux exposure. Our results indicate that enhanced atmospheric mass loss due to stellar binarity will likely not be an issue for future exoplanet-hunting missions such as the Habitable Worlds Observatory when searching for Earth-like planets around Sun-like stars.

### [4] [A change of the rotation period of asteroid (65803) Didymos caused by the DART impact](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.04793)
*Josef Durech,Petr Pravec,Masatoshi Hirabayashi,Derek C. Richardson,Harrison Agrusa,Ryota Nakano*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: NASA DART任务撞击Dimorphos后，通过分析Didymos-Dimorphos双星系统2003-2023年的光变曲线，发现主星Didymos的自转周期在撞击后变慢了0.18±0.03秒，表明撞击导致Didymos形状变得更加扁长。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究NASA DART任务撞击Dimorphos后对双星系统的影响，特别是检测主星Didymos自转周期是否因撞击而改变，以理解动能撞击技术对小行星系统的实际影响。

Method: 使用2003-2023年六个观测期的光变曲线数据，采用双小行星光变曲线分解方法，选取互掩事件之外的轨道相位部分代表主星自转光变曲线，应用光变曲线反演方法构建Didymos的凸形状模型，将撞击前后周期作为独立自由参数进行建模。

Result: 撞击前自转周期为2.2603891±0.0000002小时，撞击后为2.260440±0.000008小时，两者相差0.18±0.03秒，差异虽小但显著，表明Didymos在DART撞击后自转变慢。

Conclusion: Didymos自转周期变慢的最合理解释是撞击后Didymos形状重塑，变得更加扁长，这为动能撞击技术对小行星系统的影响提供了重要观测证据。

Abstract: On 26 September 2022, the NASA Double Asteroid Redirection Test (DART) spacecraft impacted Dimorphos, the secondary component of the binary asteroid (65803)~Didymos. This experiment tested the Kinetic Impactor technology for diverting dangerous asteroids. Due to the impact, the binary system's angular momentum has changed, resulting in a significant change in the orbital period of Dimorphos. Precise values of the pre- and post-impact orbital periods were derived from a large set of photometric light curves measured for the Didymos-Dimorphos system during six apparitions from 2003 to 2023. We used these data to detect a possible change in the rotation period of the primary as a consequence of the impact. We analyzed the binary system's light curves using the binary asteroid light curve decomposition method. We selected parts of the light curves covering orbital phases outside mutual events, which represent the primary rotational light curves. We applied the light curve inversion method to construct a convex shape model of Didymos and determine its rotation period before and after the impact. These two periods were treated as independent free parameters of the modeling. We found a value of 2.2603891 +/- 0.0000002 h for the pre-impact period and 2.260440 +/- 0.000008 h for the post-impact period. Their difference 0.18 +/- 0.03 s is small yet significant, indicating that the rotation of Didymos became slower after the DART impact. The most plausible physical explanation is Didymos's post-impact reshaping, making its shape more oblate.

### [5] [Surveying exogenous species in Saturn with ALMA I. Detecting and Mapping CO](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.05090)
*Deborah Bardet,Thierry Fouchet,Thibault Cavalié,Raphaël Moreno,Emmanuel Lellouch,Camille Lefour,Bilal Benmahi,Sandrine Guerlet*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 利用ALMA观测分析土星平流层CO分布，发现CO集中在0.1-1 mbar窄层，垂直梯度为负，经向分布均匀，不支持环物质长期输入，最符合约200年内彗星撞击来源。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 土星平流层CO的来源存在争议，可能包括内部热化学产生、彗星撞击、以及来自土星环和冰卫星的外源物质。研究旨在通过观测约束CO的垂直和经向分布，评估不同来源的相对贡献。

Method: 分析2018年5月25日ALMA高光谱分辨率观测的CO(J=3-2)谱线数据，采样范围从20°S到69°N。使用逐线辐射传输模型结合光谱反演技术反演CO垂直剖面，测试代表不同来源假设的多种先验情景。

Result: CO集中在0.1-1 mbar的窄层，具有稳健的负垂直梯度：0.1 mbar处平均丰度为(3.7±0.8)×10⁻⁸，1 mbar处为(7.2±0.9)×10⁻⁸。经向分布统计上均匀，仅在60°N附近有微弱增强（可能与土卫二有关）。未检测到显著的赤道增强。

Conclusion: 缺乏强赤道增强排除了与环物质长期稳定输入相关的来源。观测结果最符合相对近期（约200年或更年轻）的彗星撞击，其物质随后被水平混合。卡西尼号大结局期间的环物质流入要么太近期，要么效率太低，未影响探测压力层的CO丰度。

Abstract: The origin of carbon monoxide (CO) in Saturn's stratosphere remains uncertain, with proposed sources including internal thermochemical production, cometary impacts, and exogenic material from the rings and icy moons (i.e. Enceladus). We aim to constrain the vertical and meridional distribution of stratospheric CO and assess the relative contributions of these potential sources. Here, we analysed high-spectral-resolution ALMA observations of the CO (J=3-2) line obtained on 25 May 2018, sampling Saturn's limb from 20°S to 69°N. CO vertical profiles were retrieved using a line-by-line radiative transfer model combined with spectral inversion techniques, testing multiple prior scenarios representative of different source hypotheses. CO is confined to a narrow layer between 0.1 and 1 mbar, with a robust negative vertical gradient and mean abundances of (3.7+/- 0.8) x 10$^{-8}$ at 0.1 mbar and (7.2 +/- 0.9) x 10$^{-8}$ at 1 mbar. The meridional distribution is statistically homogeneous, with a marginal enhancement near 60° N plausibly related to Enceladus. No significant equatorial enhancement is detected. The absence of a strong equatorial enhancement rules out a long-lived steady source associated with ring infall. The observations are most consistent with a relatively recent ($\approx$200-year-old or younger) cometary impact whose material has since been horizontally mixed, while any Cassini Grand Finale ring influx was either too recent or inefficient to affect CO abundances at the probed pressure levels.
