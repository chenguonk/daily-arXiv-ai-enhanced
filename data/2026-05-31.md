### [1] [A Calibrated Bayesian Search for Potential Chemical Technosignatures in Polluted White Dwarf](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29811)
*Bo-Lun Huang,Zhen-Zhao Tao,Tong-Jie Zhang*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本文提出一种基于陨石校准的贝叶斯框架，用于在恒星丰度记录中搜索化学技术特征（即化学成分模式异常）。通过对3493块全岩陨石分析建立自然成分参考，对比697条恒星丰度记录与天然物质及含亲铁元素富集模板的贝叶斯证据，发现强支持人工模板的记录较少（大气模型8/697，扩散稳态子集6/148），并校准了检测所需的化学信息量（通常≥5种元素）。

Motivation: 传统寻找地外文明技术特征多聚焦于电磁信号，而化学技术特征（如恒星丰度异常）提供了一个互补的搜索维度。现有研究缺乏系统性的贝叶斯统计框架来区分天然成分与可能的人工污染（如恒星核废料倾倒）。本文旨在建立一种可校准、可量化的方法，检测恒星光谱中是否存在偏离自然岩石分布的化学模式。

Method: 1) 构建自然成分参考：基于3493块全岩陨石分析，拟合多模态自然成分分布。2) 模板定义：固定亲铁元素富集的"人工处理"模板，以Ca归一化混合分数alpha参数化。3) 贝叶斯比较：对697条恒星丰度记录，分别计算纯自然模型与"自然+模板"混合模型的贝叶斯证据。4) 校准实验：针对每条记录的元素覆盖和截断特征，进行端到端注入-恢复实验，评估判别能力。

Result: 大气光球层编译数据中：8/697条记录BF>10，4/697条BF>100。扩散调整稳态子集中：6/148条记录BF>10。推断混合模型可检测的记录比例后验中位数：大气层π̃=0.011，稳态子集π̃=0.041。判别能力通常需要≥5种检测元素，对亲铁元素模板最强的五元素组合包括Fe、Mg、Cr、Ti与Ni、Si或Na的组合。

Conclusion: 当前数据中人工模板的可检测发生率较低，但框架成功确定了候选记录并量化了不确定性。未来需要更多元素巡天观测和扩展模板家族来提升检测能力。该贝叶斯方法为化学技术特征搜索提供了统计严谨的范式，可推广至其他模板类型。

Abstract: We present a meteorite-calibrated Bayesian framework for searching archival abundance records for chemical technosignatures--operationally, compositional patterns better explained by an idealised "processed" template (endmember) than by the empirical distribution of natural rocks. We fit a multi-modal natural-composition reference using 3,493 whole-rock meteorite analyses, and for each of 697 star-paper abundance sets--spanning at least 397 distinct objects once Gaia-designated repeats are consolidated--we compare the Bayesian evidence for (i) natural material and (ii) a mixture of natural material with a fixed siderophile-enriched template, parameterised by a Ca-normalized mixing fraction alpha. Strong support for the processed template is uncommon: in the photospheric compilation (atm) 8/697 records have BF > 10 (4/697 have BF > 100), and in the diffusion-adjusted steady-state subset (acc ss; 148 records spanning at least 94 objects) 6/148 have BF > 10. We report the highest-evidence candidate records and infer the fraction of records detectably favoring the mixture model, with posterior medians pi-tilde = 0.011 (atm) and pi-tilde = 0.041 (acc ss). We calibrate the analysis with end-to-end injection-recovery experiments matched to each record's coverage and censoring. The calibration shows that discrimination is driven mainly by chemical information, typically requires greater-than-or-similar-to 5 detected elements for decisive support, and--for the siderophile template--is strongest for exact five-element panels that include Fe, Mg, Cr, and Ti together with Ni, Si, or Na. These results constrain the detectable incidence of the tested processed-composition class in current data and set observational requirements for future multi-element surveys and expanded template families.
