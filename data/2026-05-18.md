### [1] [Juno Microwave Radiometer Observations Reveal A Warmer Polar Atmosphere on Jupiter](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15367)
*Jiheng Hu,Cheng Li,Sushil K. Atreya,Leigh N. Fletcher,Eli Galanti,Tristan Guillot,Yohai Kaspi,Liming Li,Yuan Lian,Alessandro Mura,Glenn S. Orton,Fabiano A. Oyafuso,Maria Smirnova,J. Hunter Waite,Michael H. Wong,Zhimeng Zhang,Steven M. Levin,Scott J. Bolton*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本文利用朱诺号微波辐射计的高分辨率观测数据，分析了木星北极的大气垂直结构和成分，发现北极1-bar层比赤道温暖6-7K，暗示极地内部热流增强，可能与观测到的高纬度闪电活动一致。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 木星极地存在神秘的环极气旋图案，但对其垂直结构以及内部热如何塑造和维持这些系统（尤其是在没有太阳照射的条件下）的认识尚不明确，需要深入探究。

Method: 利用朱诺号微波辐射计（MWR）在11次近木点（PJ51-PJ61）获取的北纬75度以上高分辨率微波数据，通过六通道测量推导极地平均天底亮温和临边暗化光谱，并采用马尔可夫链蒙特卡洛（MCMC）反演方法进行大气廓线建模。

Result: 提出两种等可能的大气情景（干绝热含少量氨损耗或湿绝热含均匀氨）；反演得到深层氨丰度354.8+12.0/-11.0 ppmv（3±0.1倍太阳值），水丰度1.8+1.5/-1.1×1000 ppmv（2.1+1.8/-1.3倍太阳值）；发现北极1-bar层温度比赤道高6-7K（接近1σ显著性水平）。

Conclusion: 木星北极可能存在增强的内部热流向极地输送，这与高纬度地区观测到更强烈闪电活动的证据相符，为理解木星内部热循环和极区大气动力学提供了关键约束。

Abstract: The intriguing circumpolar cyclone pattern at Jupiter's poles raises fundamental questions about how these systems are organized vertically and, further, how the planet's internal heat shapes and sustains them in the absence of solar insolation. We report recent close-in observations of Jupiter's north pole acquired by NASA's Juno Microwave Radiometer (MWR), which achieved comprehensive microwave mapping of the region at an unprecedentedly high resolution. Using six-channel measurements from eleven perijove passes (PJ51-PJ61) poleward of 75N, we derive polar-mean nadir brightness temperatures and limb-darkening spectra that together point to two equally plausible atmospheric scenarios: (1) a dry-adiabatic profile with slightly depleted ammonia gas at a few bars, or (2) a moist-adiabatic profile with uniform ammonia. Markov chain Monte Carlo retrievals yield a deep ammonia abundance of 354.8+12.0/-11.0 ppmv (3+/-0.1 x solar) and a water abundance of 1.8+1.5/-1.1 x 1000 ppmv (2.1+1.8/-1.3 x solar), resembling previous estimates at lower latitudes. Remarkably, the north pole is found to be 6-7 K warmer than the equator at the 1-bar level, although the inferred difference is close to the 1-sigma uncertainty level. If confirmed, this result would suggest an enhanced internal heat flux toward the poles, which is consistent with the more intense lightning activity observed at high latitudes.

### [2] [Could life have been transferred from Mars to Earth? Laboratory and computational simulations of Martian ejecta](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15595)
*Gregory M. Davis,Jonathan Horner,Bradley D. Carter,Stephen C Marsden*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 该研究通过生物学实验和天体物理学模拟，探讨了火星喷射物将携带内生孢子的细菌运送到地球的可能性，结果表明在有利条件下，这种星际间生命物质转移是可行的。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 由于泛种论模型认为早期生命可能在地球与行星之间转移，而火星曾具备支持生命的条件，因此探索早期生命从火星到地球的星际转移可能性具有重要意义。

Method: 对内生孢子（由裂解细菌菌落保护）进行不同旋转模式下的UVC辐照实验，模拟星际暴露条件；同时进行多体动力学模拟，研究火星近日点和远日点喷射的粒子到达地球的时间尺度。

Result: 火星喷射物可在数年内到达地球，若在理想时间喷射，转移时间甚至可缩短至一年以内；受保护的孢子能够抵御星际辐射环境。

Conclusion: 在有利条件下，从火星到地球的星际间生物活性物质转移是可行的，支持了早期生命可能通过火星喷射物抵达地球的假说。

Abstract: The study of the origin of life on Earth has been broadened due to panspermia models that suggest that early life may have been transferred between planets. Mars likely once had conditions that could support life, and it is interesting therefore to consider the question of early interplanetary transfer of life from Mars to the Earth. Endospore forming bacteria are ideal candidates for these studies as they can withstand harsh environmental conditions. For this reason, the idea that early life could have been delivered to Earth on Martian ejecta in the late Hadean period has gained considerable interest. To assess this, we have performed a series of both biological and astrophysical experiments. We exposed endospores shielded by a lysed colony of bacteria to extended UVC irradiation under a variety of rotation regimes, to simulate interplanetary exposure on ejecta with a variety of rotation periods. We also performed detailed n-body simulations of particles ejected from Mars at both perihelion and aphelion, finding that Martian ejecta can reach the Earth on timescales of just a few years - suggesting that, with ejection at the ideal time, transfer could occur within one year. Taken together, this study suggests this interplanetary transfer of biologically viable material from Mars to Earth is plausible under favourable conditions.
