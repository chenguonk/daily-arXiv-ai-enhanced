### [1] [Worlds Next Door. III. Indirect Evidence for Enhanced Atmospheric Metallicity and/or the Presence of Water Clouds in the Nearest Jupiter-analog $ε$ Eri b](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23423)
*Aniket Sanghi,James Mang,Jorge Llop-Sayson,Eric Mamajek,William Thompson,Ankan Sur,Charles Beichman,Geoffrey Bryden,Andras Gaspar,Jarron Leisenring,Dimitri Mawet,Caroline Morley,Jean-Baptiste Ruffio,Schuyler Wolff,Marie Ygouf*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: JWST/NIRCam对最近（3.2 pc）的木星类似系外行星ε Eri b进行了最灵敏的直接成像搜索，在4-5 μm波段达到前所未有的对比度，但未探测到行星。通过更新的恒星年龄（11亿年）和大气模型分析，发现ε Eri b可能具有类似木星的富金属大气或水冰云，或者质量小于0.81 M_Jup。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: ε Eri b是距离地球最近（3.2 pc）的木星类似系外行星，对其直接成像研究有助于理解类木行星的形成和演化。JWST的高灵敏度为探测这类行星提供了前所未有的机会。

Method: 使用JWST/NIRCam日冕仪在4-5 μm（F444W）波段进行直接成像观测，达到5σ对比灵敏度≈3.0×10^{-7}（Δ≈16.3 mag）。更新恒星年龄至1.1±0.1 Gyr，使用无云Sonora Flame Skimmer模型和自定义PICASO斑块云模型分析大气特性。

Result: 未探测到ε Eri b的直接成像信号。F444W非探测结果可通过富金属大气和/或含水冰云的大气解释，表明其大气与木星相似。如果不考虑动力学质量约束，质量≲0.81 M_Jup、太阳金属丰度的无云行星也与观测上限一致。

Conclusion: ε Eri b可能具有类似木星的富金属大气或水冰云，或者质量小于预期。未来JWST、Roman日冕仪、Lazuli天文台和EELT/METIS的观测将提供进一步探测机会。

Abstract: We present the most sensitive direct imaging search for the nearest ($d = 3.2$ pc) Jupiter-analog exoplanet, $ε$ Eri b, with JWST/NIRCam coronagraphy between 4-5 $μ$m (F444W). We achieve a 5$σ$ contrast sensitivity $\approx3.0\times10^{-7}$ ($Δ\approx 16.3$ mag) in the F444W filter at the expected planet separation of $\approx$1". This is the deepest 4-5 $μ$m contrast performance achieved for any JWST/NIRCam observation to date at these separations (and $>10\times$ better than ground-based limits). Yet, the planet remains elusive to imaging. We update the star's age to $1.1\pm0.1$ Gyr, older than previous age estimates, using the latest gyrochronology relations. This significantly impacts $ε$ Eri b's inferred effective temperature ($T_{\rm eff}$), which is now expected to lie between 150-200 K based on evolutionary models for a 1 $M_{\rm Jup}$ planet. Using cloud-free Sonora Flame Skimmer models and custom PICASO patchy cloud models in the above $T_{\rm eff}$ range, we find that the F444W non-detection of $ε$ Eri b can be explained by a metal-enriched atmosphere and/or an atmosphere containing water ice clouds. Both possibilities suggest that $ε$ Eri b's atmosphere is strikingly similar to that of Jupiter in our Solar System. Alternatively, if we do not enforce the dynamical mass ($0.98 \pm 0.09\;M_{\rm Jup}$), a solar metallicity, cloud-free, $\lesssim0.81\;M_{\rm Jup}$ planet would be consistent with the NIRCam upper limit based on the Sonora Flame Skimmer evolutionary models. Finally, we place limits on the size of a potential ring system using the NIRCam/F210M data and discuss the opportunity to directly image $ε$ Eri b with additional JWST observations, the Roman Coronagraph Instrument, the ExtraSolar Coronagraph on the Lazuli Observatory, and EELT/METIS.

### [2] [Contemporaneous Optical and Near-Infrared Observations of the Interstellar Comet 3I/ATLAS Pre- and Post-Perihelion](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23586)
*Kyle Medler,Willem B. Hoogendam,Christopher Ashall,Bin Yang,James J. Wray,Benjamin J. Shappee,Karen J. Meech,Michael A. Tucker,Katie Auchettl,Dhvanil D. Desai,Jason T. Hinkle,Andrew M. Hoffman,Mark E. Huber,David O. Jones,Ruining Zhao*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 对星际天体3I/ATLAS进行多波段光谱观测，发现其光谱斜率在光学和近红外区域呈现不同特征，检测到多种元素发射，并与2I/Borisov的光谱特征进行比较。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 星际天体为研究其他恒星系统的形成提供了独特视角。本文旨在通过对星际天体3I/ATLAS的光谱观测，了解其组成和物理特性。

Method: 使用Keck-I/LRIS、Keck-II/NIRES、Gemini/GMOS和UH88/SNIFS等光谱仪，在日心距离3.7-1.8天文单位范围内，对3I/ATLAS进行0.3-2.5μm波长范围的光谱观测。

Result: 检测到Ni、CN、Fe和弱C3发射；光学区域光谱斜率为正（蓝区21-27%，红区6-10%），近红外区域为负（-0.9%和-2.3%）；在约1.1μm处发现光谱形状转折；近红外水特征深度未增加；与2I/Borisov的光谱形状相似。

Conclusion: 3I/ATLAS的光谱特征表明其具有复杂的组成和物理特性，与另一个星际天体2I/Borisov相似，为理解星际天体的性质提供了重要数据。

Abstract: Interstellar objects provide a unique view into the formation of other star systems. Here we present spectroscopic observations of the recently discovered interstellar object 3I/ATLAS between a heliocentric distance of $3.7$ to $1.8$~au on either side of its travels through perihelion. We obtained several observations with the Keck-I/LRIS, Keck-II/NIRES, Gemini/GMOS, and UH88/SNIFS spectrographs, covering a wavelength range of $0.3 - 2.5~\mathrm{μm}$. We report the continued emission of both Ni and CN, along with post-perihelion detections of Fe and a weak detection of $\mathrm{C_3}$. We determine the spectral slope across optical and NIR wavelengths and find a positive spectral slope in the optical, with values ranging from $\sim 21 - 27\%$ in the blue regions ($0.4 - 0.55~\mathrm{μm}$) to $\sim 6 - 10\%$ in the red ($0.65 - 0.9~\mathrm{μm}$) regions. In contrast, the NIR showed a negative spectral slope of $\sim -0.9 \%$ between $0.9 - 1.5~\mathrm{μm}$ and $\sim -2.3\%$ between $1.9 - 2.5~\mathrm{μm}$. 3I/ATLAS shows a clear turnover in its spectral shape at $\sim 1.1~\mathrm{μm}$, corresponding to scattered light from the dusty coma. Finally, in the NIR, we do not find an increase in the depth of the water features identified in an earlier NIR observation of 3I/ATLAS. Our observations of 3I/ATLAS in the NIR show a similar shape to the NIR spectrum of 2I/Borisov as it approached perihelion.

### [3] [Active Learning for Planet Habitability Classification under Extreme Class Imbalance](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23666)
*R. I. El-Kholy,Z. M. Hayman*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 使用基于池的主动学习改进系外行星宜居性分类效率，在标签不平衡和观测资源有限的情况下，通过不确定性采样减少标注需求，并识别出值得进一步研究的候选行星。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 系外行星目录规模增大和异质性增强使得系统性宜居性评估变得困难，特别是潜在宜居行星极其稀少且标签不断演变。需要更高效的分类方法来应对标签不平衡、信息不完整和观测资源有限的挑战。

Method: 从宜居世界目录和NASA系外行星档案构建统一数据集，将宜居性评估作为二分类问题。建立基于梯度提升决策树的监督基线模型，优先考虑稀有潜在宜居行星的识别。然后将该模型嵌入主动学习框架，比较基于不确定性的边界采样与随机查询在不同标注预算下的表现。

Result: 主动学习显著减少了达到监督性能所需的标注实例数量，显示出明显的标签效率提升。通过集成独立训练的主动学习模型的预测，使用平均概率和不确定性对原始标记为非宜居的行星进行排序，识别出一个值得进一步研究的稳健候选行星。

Conclusion: 主动学习为在标签不平衡、信息不完整和观测资源有限的数据环境中指导宜居性研究提供了一个原则性框架，支持保守的、不确定性感知的后续目标优先排序而非推测性重新分类。

Abstract: The increasing size and heterogeneity of exoplanet catalogs have made systematic habitability assessment challenging, particularly given the extreme scarcity of potentially habitable planets and the evolving nature of their labels. In this study, we explore the use of pool-based active learning to improve the efficiency of habitability classification under realistic observational constraints. We construct a unified dataset from the Habitable World Catalog and the NASA Exoplanet Archive and formulate habitability assessment as a binary classification problem. A supervised baseline based on gradient-boosted decision trees is established and optimized for recall in order to prioritize the identification of rare potentially habitable planets. This model is then embedded within an active learning framework, where uncertainty-based margin sampling is compared against random querying across multiple runs and labeling budgets. We find that active learning substantially reduces the number of labeled instances required to approach supervised performance, demonstrating clear gains in label efficiency. To connect these results to a practical astronomical use case, we aggregate predictions from independently trained active-learning models into an ensemble and use the resulting mean probabilities and uncertainties to rank planets originally labeled as non-habitable. This procedure identifies a single robust candidate for further study, illustrating how active learning can support conservative, uncertainty-aware prioritization of follow-up targets rather than speculative reclassification. Our results indicate that active learning provides a principled framework for guiding habitability studies in data regimes characterized by label imbalance, incomplete information, and limited observational resources.

### [4] [Linear Polarization Variations and Circular Polarization are Common Among Airless Bodies](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.24196)
*Sloane J. Wiktorowicz,Amanda J. Bayless,Larissa A. Nofi*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 研究发现小行星和近地天体在旋转过程中普遍存在与相位锁定的线性偏振变化，这些变化由表面反照率和成分异质性引起。近地天体Didymos的偏振变化特别显著，可能与DART撞击产生的喷射物有关。同时发现低反照率M型小行星的光学圆偏振增强，可作为识别金属富集天体的新方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究小行星和近地天体的偏振特性，以了解其表面成分异质性和物理特性，特别是探索偏振变化作为探测表面异质性的工具，以及光学圆偏振作为识别金属富集小行星的新方法。

Method: 使用Lick天文台Shane 3-m和Nickel 1-m望远镜上的POLISH2偏振仪，在383-720 nm波段对多个小行星和近地天体进行长期（八年）偏振观测，分析其线性偏振和圆偏振的变化特征。

Result: 几乎所有研究天体（包括1 Ceres、2 Pallas等12个天体）都显示出统计显著的、可重复的线性偏振变化（≥0.01%）。其中Hebe、Victoria和Didymos的偏振变化比已知的Vesta大1.5-3.5倍。Didymos的巨大变化可能与DART撞击产生的去偏振喷射物有关。同时发现低反照率M型小行星的光学圆偏振增强，与高雷达反照率相关。

Conclusion: 小行星普遍存在与旋转相位锁定的偏振变化，这反映了表面成分和反照率的异质性。近地天体可能具有更大的偏振变化。光学圆偏振增强可作为识别金属富集小行星的有效替代方法，为小行星表面特性研究提供了新工具。

Abstract: Using the POLISH2 polarimeter at the Lick Observatory Shane 3-m and Nickel 1-m telescopes, we discover rotation phase-locked variations in linear polarization to be common among asteroids and a NEO in a clear, 383 to 720 nm bandpass. Essentially all bodies in our eight-year study harbor statistically significant, repeatable linear polarization variations at the $0.01\% = 100$ ppm level or above (1 Ceres, 2 Pallas, 3 Juno, 4 Vesta, 6 Hebe, 7 Iris, 12 Victoria, 15 Eunomia, 16 Psyche, 132 Aethra, 216 Kleopatra, and 65803 Didymos). Since polarimetry is a differential technique, such variations cannot be due to shape changes and must be caused by heterogeneity in surface albedo and/or composition. While (4) Vesta has long been known to exhibit large, repeatable polarization variations across its surface, we discover the variations on (6) Hebe, (12) Victoria, and (65803) Didymos to be 1.5 to 3.5 times as large. We hypothesize that the \textit{DART} impact with Dimorphos blanketed Didymos with depolarizing ejecta, which suggests pristine variations across Didymos to have been even larger. As the only NEO in this study with data quality sufficient to investigate polarization variations, Didymos' huge variations suggest they may be common among NEOs. We also discover optical circular polarization to be enhanced for low-albedo, M type asteroids, which is correlated with their large radar albedos. Thus, we present optical circular polarimetry as an alternative method for the identification of metalliferous bodies.
