### [1] [Unusually Hot Interiors Could Reconcile the Missing Methane Problem for Warm-to-Hot Exoplanets with Hydrogen Atmospheres](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01672)
*Xinting Yu,Christopher R. Glein,Daniel P. Thorngren,David F. Murray*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: JWST观测显示许多系外行星大气中甲烷含量异常低，研究者提出一个简单的地球化学框架，通过分析CO-CH4和CO-CO2化学平衡来估算行星内部温度，发现部分行星需要比标准演化模型预测更高的内部温度。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: JWST提供了系外行星大气化学成分的精确数据，但观测显示许多氢主导大气中的温暖至炎热系外行星缺乏预期的甲烷含量。研究者希望探索是否升高的内部温度可以解释这种甲烷缺失现象。

Method: 开发了一个简单快速的分析框架，专注于CO-CH4和CO-CO2两个关键化学平衡，而不是使用计算昂贵的正向网格模型。通过JWST观测到的H2O、CO、CH4和CO2丰度，快速约束与观测一致的最小内部温度。

Result: 应用于12个温暖至炎热系外行星，发现多个目标需要的最小内部温度超过标准演化模型的预测，而其他目标则与较低内部温度解一致或存在与其他解的简并性。部分行星明显偏离了从热木星质量-半径群体推导的经验平衡温度-内部温度关系。

Conclusion: 大气化学可以作为系外行星内部的补充探测工具。虽然欧姆耗散可能是普遍群体的重要热源，但某些行星可能需要额外的加热过程（如潮汐加热）。甲烷缺失现象暗示了比预期更热的行星内部。

Abstract: JWST is revolutionizing the field of exoplanet atmospheres by delivering unprecedented spectroscopic constraints on their chemical compositions. It has provided tight constraints on the abundances of dominant carbon- and oxygen-bearing species on numerous warm-to-hot exoplanets with hydrogen-dominated atmospheres. Under thermochemical equilibrium, many of these exoplanets should have abundant methane (CH4); however, CH4 has, so far, only been spotted in a few cases. Here, we present a simple, geochemistry-inspired framework to explore whether elevated intrinsic temperatures (T$\rm_{int}$) can account for the CH4 depletions. Instead of using computationally expensive, forward grid models, our fast analytical framework focuses on two key chemical equilibria: CO-CH4 and CO-CO2, allowing us to quickly constrain the minimum Tint that is consistent with JWST-observed abundances of H2O, CO, CH4, and CO2. Applying this framework to 12 warm-to-hot exoplanets, we find that several targets require minimum Tint values exceeding those predicted by standard evolution models, while others remain consistent with lower Tint solutions or exhibit degeneracies with other solutions. Our sample enables an initial exploration of population-level trends: while many exoplanets broadly follow an empirical Teq-Tint relation derived from the hot Jupiter mass-radius population, a subset of targets lie well above this trend. The apparent need for hotter interiors suggests that while Ohmic dissipation is probably an important heat source among the general population, additional heating processes, such as tidal heating, may also play important roles for some planets. Our results demonstrate the diagnostic power of atmospheric chemistry as a complementary probe of exoplanet interiors.

### [2] [Hot Rocks Survey V: Secondary Eclipse Photometry of GJ 3473 b with JWST/MIRI](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02332)
*Måns Holmberg,Hannah Diamond-Lowe,João M. Mendonça,Daniel Kitzmann,Néstor Espinoza,Natalie H. Allen,Prune C. August,Mark Fortune,Amélie Gressier,Jegug Ih,Erik Meier Valdés,Merlin Zgraggen,Lars A. Buchhave,Brice-Olivier Demory,Chloe Fisher,Neale P. Gibson,Kevin Heng,Bibiana Prinoth,Adam J. Burgasser*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: JWST首次观测到岩石系外行星GJ 3473 b的二次食，探测到平均深度186±45 ppm的日食信号，数据既符合无大气岩石表面模型，也符合稀薄大气模型，但排除了厚CO₂大气（表面压力上限1.2-6.5 bar）。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究高度辐照的岩石行星是否能保留大气层，还是会被恒星辐射剥离，这是系外行星研究中的一个关键未解问题，需要通过观测来绘制这一边界。

Method: 使用JWST的MIRI F1500W光度计对GJ 3473 b进行四次观测，获得二次食数据。测试了广泛的数据处理和分析假设，研究了MIRI探测器沉降行为。建模了多种无大气表面（不同成分、纹理、空间风化程度）和理想化大气情景（包括大气坍缩可能性）。

Result: 探测到平均深度186±45 ppm的日食信号，低于黑体预期值。数据与大气和裸岩解释都一致，但排除了厚CO₂大气（95%置信度上限1.2-6.5 bar表面压力）。发现日食深度可能存在访次间变化（33-371 ppm），但需要更多数据确认。

Conclusion: MIRI F1500W日食测量在解释岩石系外行星时存在挑战和固有简并性，仅凭此类观测可能无法唯一区分裸岩和大气情景。未来需要光谱或相位曲线观测来确定GJ 3473 b是否拥有实质性大气层。

Abstract: JWST is transforming our ability to characterise small exoplanets, from sub-Neptunes to rocky worlds. A key open question is whether highly irradiated rocky planets can retain atmospheres or are stripped bare by stellar irradiation -- a boundary that remains to be mapped observationally. Here we present the first JWST secondary eclipse observations of the rocky exoplanet GJ 3473 b, obtained with MIRI F1500W photometry. Using four visits, we confidently detect the eclipse at an average depth of 186$\pm$45 ppm, somewhat lower than expected for a blackbody. We test a wide range of data reduction and analysis assumptions and provide new insights into MIRI detector settling behaviour that will benefit future observations. We model a suite of airless surfaces with varied compositions, textures, and degrees of space weathering, as well as idealised atmospheric scenarios including the possibility of atmospheric collapse. Both atmospheric and bare-rock interpretations remain consistent with the data, but we exclude thick CO$_2$ atmospheres, placing a 95 % credible upper limit of 1.2-6.5 bar on the surface pressure. We also find tentative evidence for visit-to-visit variability in eclipse depth (33-371 ppm), though additional data are required to confirm this. Our results highlight the challenges and intrinsic degeneracies in interpreting MIRI F1500W eclipse measurements of rocky exoplanets, indicating that such observations alone may not uniquely distinguish between bare-rock and atmospheric scenarios. Future spectroscopic or phase-curve observations will be required to determine whether or not GJ 3473 b hosts a substantial atmosphere
