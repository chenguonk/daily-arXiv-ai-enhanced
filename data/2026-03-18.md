### [1] [Inferring the mass and size of 3I/ATLAS from its non-gravitational acceleration](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.15735)
*Valentin Thoss,Abraham Loeb,Andreas Burkert*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 通过结合水冰和二氧化碳升华模型，分析星际天体3I/ATLAS的非引力参数，估算其核半径约0.42-1.15公里，质量约1.6×10¹¹-3.2×10¹²千克，表明二氧化碳主导表面升华，水升华主要发生在彗发中。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 观测发现3I/ATLAS在近日点附近有强烈气体和尘埃产生，并伴随快速增亮。其核外逸气体导致可探测的非引力加速度，需要结合质量损失率模型来估算该星际天体的质量和大小。

Method: 结合水冰和二氧化碳升华模型推导非引力参数，并考虑升华所需活性表面积对核大小的约束。通过分析非引力加速度的标度关系（a_ng(r)∼1/r²）来验证模型一致性。

Result: 若质量损失主要由CO₂升华主导，核半径约0.42公里，质量约1.6×10¹¹千克（假设密度0.5 g/cm³，不对称因子0.5）。若考虑水升华，核半径0.74-1.15公里，质量8.5-32×10¹¹千克，但与火箭效应估计存在矛盾。

Conclusion: 结果表明，3I/ATLAS的大部分水升华发生在彗发中，而CO₂主导表面升华。获得的核半径远小于最近的光度测量估计值（约1.3公里），这可能是因为CO₂产量高于观测值，或密度显著低于假设值。较轻的核质量可能与其源自贫金属环境一致。

Abstract: Observations of the interstellar object 3I/ATLAS have revealed a strong production of gas and dust near perihelion, together with rapid brightening. The outgassing from the nucleus has led to a detectable non-gravitational acceleration. In this work, we combine models of the mass loss rate of water and carbon dioxide to derive the non-gravitational parameters and estimate the mass and size of 3I/ATLAS. Therefore, our results indicate that a large fraction of water sublimation is occurring in the coma and that CO$_2$ dominates sublimation on the surface. An overall lighter nucleus of 3I/ATLAS might be favored based on its recently claimed origin from a metal-poor environment and the corresponding mass budget of interstellar objects.
