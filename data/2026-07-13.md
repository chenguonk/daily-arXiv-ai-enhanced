### [1] [Tentative detection of circularly polarized bursty radio emissions from the HD 189733 exoplanetary system using NenuFAR beamformed observations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08910)
*Jake D. Turner,Philippe Zarka,Jean-Mathias Grießmeier,Corentin K. Louis,Xiang Zhang,Emilie Mauduit,Tomoki Kimura*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本文利用NenuFAR射电望远镜对HD 189733系外行星系统的波束合成观测数据，独立验证了此前Zhang等人（2025）在50 MHz探测到的圆偏振爆发射电信号。研究在成像观测发现爆发前约1小时探测到了类似信号（~10σ），但信号是否来自天体物理源仍存在不确定性，部分归因于过量相关噪声。若确为天体物理起源，最可能解释为行星极光辐射，但无法完全排除恒星活动可能性。

Motivation: 探测系外行星的极光射电辐射是揭示其磁场、内部结构、大气性质及宜居性的重要手段。Zhang等人（2025）首次在HD 189733系统探测到疑似信号，但其来源未知（可能是行星极光、星-行星相互作用、恒星活动或M矮星伴星）。本研究旨在通过独立的波束合成观测数据对该信号进行交叉验证，以确定其真实性及起源。

Method: 使用NenuFAR望远镜对HD 189733系统进行同步波束合成观测，采用BOREALIS数据降维流程处理数据，分析圆偏振爆发性射电信号，并与成像观测结果进行时间比对和特征对比。

Result: 在Zhang等人（2025）成像探测到的爆发前约1小时，独立探测到圆偏振爆发性射电信号（显著性~10σ）。然而，信号存在过量相关噪声，其天体物理起源仍存疑。若确为天体物理信号，观测特征最符合行星极光起源，但恒星活动无法完全排除。

Conclusion: 需要更多低频射电观测来确认信号的天体物理性质，并搜寻HD 189733射电信号的周期性以确定其真正来源。本研究展示了同步波束合成与成像观测相结合在搜寻系外行星射电辐射中的强大潜力。

Abstract: Observing auroral radio emission is one of the most promising methods for detecting exoplanetary magnetic fields, which provide valuable insights into planetary interiors, atmospheric properties, and potential habitability. The first hints of exoplanet auroral emission are starting to emerge. Recently, Zhang et al. (2025) reported a detection at 50 MHz of a circularly polarized bursty emission from the HD 189733 exoplanetary system using NenuFAR low-frequency imaging observations. The source of the emission is still unknown and may be caused by planetary auroral emissions, star-planet interactions, stellar activity, or the M-dwarf stellar companion. In this study, we analyze beamformed observations from NenuFAR of HD 189733 taken simultaneously during the previously detected burst. This dataset allows for an independent verification of the detected burst with a different backend and processing steps. Using the BOREALIS data reduction pipeline, we tentatively detect circularly polarized bursty emission ($\sim$10$σ$) from HD 189733 $\sim$1 hour before the burst found from the imaging observations. However, some uncertainty remains on whether our detected signal is astrophysical in nature due to excess correlated noise. Assuming an astrophysical origin, our observed characteristics are most consistent with a planetary origin, but stellar emission cannot be completely ruled. Therefore, more low-frequency radio observations are needed to confirm the astrophysical nature of our signal and to search for periodicity in the radio signal from HD 189733 to determine the true cause of the emission. These observations are ongoing. Our study highlights the power of simultaneous beamformed and imaging observations in the search for radio emission from exoplanets.

### [2] [The GAPS Programme with HARPS-N at TNG LXXVII. Occurrence rates of small close-in planets in the presence of cold Jupiters](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.09320)
*A. Ruggieri,S. Desidera,M. Pinamonti,D. Barbato,A. Sozzetti,A. S. Bonomo,L. Naponiello,M. Damasso,S. Benatti,I. Carleo,R. Gratton,K. Biazzo,A. F. Lanza,G. Piccinini,N. Nari,G. Mantovan,J. Maldonado,D. Nardiello,A. Fiorenzano,A. Ghedina,A. Bignamini*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本研究通过分析137颗已知拥有冷木星（CJ）的恒星，构建了大规模的均匀样本，评估了内太阳系小行星（ISP）的存在率及其与冷木星的相关性。研究发现，热、温、冷海王星的存在率分别为5%、13%和12%；热、温超级地球的存在率分别为11%和16%。动态稳定的内部区域更有利于小行星存在。结果表明，在平均恒星金属度和质量条件下，ISP与CJ之间没有强相关性，但热木星可能与外部巨星更常见地关联。

Motivation: 探究内太阳系小行星（ISP）与外冷巨行星之间是否存在相关性，以评估类似太阳系（内小行星+外巨行星）结构在宇宙中的普遍性。

Method: 选取137颗已知拥有冷木星（a>1au, msini>0.1M_J）的恒星（包含23颗也拥有热木星的恒星），整合多种仪器的数据（包括GAPS项目中HARPS-N的未发表数据），使用PyORBIT进行统一拟合。通过RV探测图计算ISP发生率，并使用两个独立代码进行交叉验证。按系统参数将样本分为子组评估影响。

Result: 确认了137个系统中213颗已知行星，并识别出6个新候选者。按质量和周期分类：海王星（热5%、温13%、冷12%），超级地球（热11%、温16%）。动态稳定的系统中小行星发生率更高。未发现ISP与CJ间强相关性，但HJ可能更常与外部巨星关联。

Conclusion: 在平均恒星金属度和质量条件下，内小行星与外冷巨行星之间不存在强相关性，但热木星可能与外部巨行星更常见地共存，这为理解行星系统结构多样性提供了重要统计依据。

Abstract: Context. The architecture of our Solar System, with inner small planets (ISPs) and outer giants, may or may not be common. Understanding whether a correlation exists between ISPs and outer cold giants is key to evaluating how common systems with a similar architecture to our own are. Aims. This study aims to build a large, homogeneous sample of systems hosting cold Jupiters (CJs, a > 1 au, msini > 0.1 M$_J$) detected via radial velocities (RVs), and to assess the presence of additional ISPs (P < 400 d, 3 < msini < 31.7 M$_{\oplus}$), studying the correlation between these two types of objects. Methods. We selected 137 stars known to host a CJ, including 23 which also harbor a hot Jupiter and were treated separately. Data from various instruments were compiled, including unpublished data gathered with HARPS-N within the GAPS program, and consistently fitted using PyORBIT. We derived RV detection maps and calculated occurrence rates for ISPs, cross-validating results with two independent codes. The sample was divided into subgroups to evaluate how system parameters influence planet occurrence. Results. We confirmed the 213 already known planets in the 137 systems and also identified six new candidates. We divided them, based on mass and period, into Neptunes (10 < m sin i < 31.7 M$_{\oplus}$) and Super-Earths (3 < m sin i < 10 M$_{\oplus}$), and into hot (1 < P < 10 d), warm (10 < P < 100 d), and cool (100 < P < 400 d). We found occurrences of 5%, 13%, and 12% for hot, warm, and cool Neptunes, respectively, and 11% and 16% for hot and warm Super-Earths, respectively. Systems with dynamically stable inner regions show higher rates of small planets. These findings are consistent with previous studies showing no strong correlation between ISPs and CJs at average stellar metallicity and mass, and suggest that HJs may be more commonly associated with external giants.

### [3] [Companion Architectures of Sub-Saturns: Distinct Migration Pathways Across the Neptunian Landscape](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.09451)
*Luis Thomas,Louise D. Nielsen,Alex J. Cridland,Martin Schlecker,Sydney Vach*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 研究发现，近距亚土星（Neptunian desert和ridge）与远距亚土星（savanna）在伴星结构上存在显著差异：savanna亚土星中69.9%有近邻伴星，64.4%处于多行星系统；而desert/ridge亚土星仅10.5%有近邻伴星，14.9%有多行星系统。这支持了两种迁移通道：高偏心率迁移（HEM）形成desert/ridge，宁静盘迁移或原位形成填充savanna。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 验证亚土星是否具有系统性的不同伴星结构，以检验Neptunian desert和ridge的行星是否通过高偏心率迁移到达，而savanna行星是否通过宁静盘迁移形成。

Method: 编译86个同时具有凌星和径向速度数据的系统，构建完备性地图并合并为伴星探测概率面，利用Poisson-Binomial框架计算不同类型伴星的occurrence rates。

Result: savanna亚土星中69.9%有近邻伴星（P<200 d），64.4%处于多行星系统；desert/ridge亚土星仅10.5%有近邻伴星，14.9%有多行星系统。两类系统中存在的近邻伴星几乎都是小质量（M<20 M⊕）。这些差异对亚土星半径、密度、偏心率和宿主星属性等交叉检验均稳健。

Conclusion: Desert/ridge亚土星与热木星的伴星结构相似（动力学排空系统），savanna亚土星与暖木星相似（紧凑多行星系统）。这支持同一行星群中存在两种迁移通道：HEM将行星送至desert/ridge，宁静盘迁移或原位形成填充savanna。

Abstract: Close-in sub-Saturns (4 - 8.5 R$_\oplus$) are depleted in the Neptunian desert, accumulate in a narrow overdensity near P = 3.2 - 5.7 d (the Neptunian ridge), and thin out into the more moderately populated savanna at longer periods. We test whether sub-Saturns have systematically different companion architectures, as predicted if desert and ridge planets arrived through high-eccentricity migration while savanna planets migrated quiescently. We compile 86 systems with both transit and RV data, construct completeness maps and combine them into detection probability surfaces for companions. The combined completeness maps are used to calculate companion occurrence rates across different companion types with a Poisson-Binomial framework. Companion architectures differ significantly across the landscape. $69.9_{-7.7}^{+6.9}\%$ of savanna sub-Saturns have nearby companions (P < 200 d) compared to only $10.5_{-5.3}^{+7.8}\%$ of desert and ridge sub-Saturns. Additionally, sub-Saturns in the savanna often reside in multi-planet systems with $64.4_{-9.8}^{+8.9}\%$ having more than one companion planet versus only $14.9_{-9.1}^{+13.7}\%$ in the desert and ridge. In both populations, the nearby companions that do exist are almost exclusively small (M < 20 M$_\oplus$), meaning the sub-Saturn is typically the dominant body of its inner system. These contrasts are robust to crosscuts in sub-Saturn radius, bulk density, eccentricity, and host-star properties. Desert and ridge sub-Saturns reside in dynamically emptied systems whose nearby companion rates match those of hot Jupiters, while savanna sub-Saturns inhabit compact multi-planet systems resembling those of warm Jupiters. This parallel supports two migration channels operating within a single population: HEM delivering planets to the desert and ridge, and quiescent disk migration or in-situ formation populating the savanna.

### [4] [Beyond the Cube: Overlapping Grid Methods for Debris Collision Risk Assessment](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.09634)
*Yacob Medhin,Simone Servadio*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本文提出双立方体（DC）方法，通过空间偏移的次级网格恢复轨道碎片模拟中被立方体方法遗漏的跨边界交会事件，在保持O(N)复杂度下将盲视率从9.70%降至4.21%，并发现立方体方法存在系统性高估偏差。基于DSMC类比推导的幂律校正将校准误差从12.9%降至1.9%，而基于距离分布几何的无参数高斯校正实现0.08%的残差。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 立方体方法在轨道碎片模拟中虽然将交会筛检复杂度降至O(N)，但存在"边界盲视"缺陷：当两个物体恰好位于网格边界两侧时，系统会将其碰撞概率错误地赋值为零，导致大量真实交会事件被遗漏。

Method: 提出双立方体（DC）方法：通过仅使用网格索引查找的空间偏移次级网格来恢复跨边界交会，保持O(N)复杂度。进一步推导两种校正：基于DSMC动力学理论类比的幂律校正，以及基于对距离分布几何的无参数高斯校正。

Result: DC方法在8000个蒙特卡洛种子验证下将盲视率从9.70%降至4.21%；同步实验确认残余盲视为时间起源（0.00%）。幂律校正将校准误差从12.9%降至1.9%（k=1）和4.0%（k=2）；高斯校正达到0.08%的残余误差。

Conclusion: 双立方体方法有效缓解了边界盲视问题，并揭示了立方体公式中系统性高估偏差的存在。两种独立校正方法均显著提升了碰撞预测精度，已集成到MOCAT-MC中。

Abstract: The cube method reduces conjunction screening in orbital debris simulations to $\mathcal{O}(N)$ cost by evaluating only object pairs sharing the same grid cell at each snapshot, but systematically assigns zero collision probability to pairs separated by a cell boundary at that epoch, a failure known as boundary blindness. This paper introduces the Double Cube (DC) method, which recovers boundary-crossing conjunctions through a spatially shifted secondary grid using bin-index lookup alone, preserving $\mathcal{O}(N)$ complexity. Validated across 8,000 Monte Carlo seeds, DC reduces the blindness rate from $β_{\mathrm{Cube}} = 9.70\%$ to $β_{\mathrm{DC}} = 4.21\%$; a synchronized experiment confirms the residual is temporal in origin by reaching exactly $0.00\%$. Removing blindness reveals a systematic per-pair overestimation in the cube formula that blind zero-probability assignments had been masking, suppressing the overall predicted collision rate below the true rate. Two independent corrections are derived and validated: a power-law correction motivated by the Direct Simulation Monte Carlo kinetic theory analogy reduces the calibration error from $12.9\%$ to $1.9\%$ at $k = 1$ and $4.0\%$ at $k = 2$, bracketing perfect calibration from opposite sides; a parameter-free Gaussian correction derived from the pair-distance distribution geometry achieves a residual of $0.08\%$. Both corrections have been implemented in MOCAT-MC.

### [5] [HD 148797: A bright F-type star with two moderate-period low-density sub-Jovian planets. Compact multi-planet architectures are common in the Neptunian savanna](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.09656)

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 该研究确认并表征了两颗围绕明亮F型恒星HD 148797运行的亚木星行星（HD 148797 b和c），利用TESS和CHEOPS测光数据确定了它们的轨道周期（42.1天和68.2天，周期比接近黄金分割1.619）、半径（约8.3 RE）、凌星时间变化反演得到的质量（约39 ME）和低密度（约0.38 g/cm³），并发现这类"稀树草原"亚木星多行星系统通常处于动力学冷系统中。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 确认中等轨距亚木星的存在并表征其物理性质，研究这类行星的群体特性与系统架构，为行星形成与迁移机制提供观测约束。

Method: 使用TESS和CHEOPS测光时间序列确定轨道周期和半径；通过探测显著的反相关凌星时间变化（TTVs）并结合光动力学建模反演行星质量；统计分析稀树草原（Neptunian savanna）区域的多行星系统分数和相邻行星周期比。

Abstract: We report the confirmation and characterisation of two moderate-period sub-Jovian planets transiting the bright F-type star HD 148797 (G=9.4 mag, Teff=6441 +/- 51 K). Using photometric time series from TESS and CHEOPS, we determine orbital periods of 42.1 d for HD 148797 b and 68.2 d for HD 148797 c, putting the period ratio very close to the golden mean at 1.619 and therefore near several strong harmonics, and measure planetary radii of 8.25 +/- 0.37 RE and 8.37 +/- 0.38 RE, respectively. This pattern suggests that savanna sub-Jovians are commonly found in dynamically cold systems, consistent with smoother migration pathways such as disk-driven migration rather than disruptive high-eccentricity tidal migration. As a bright, co-evolved system hosting two warm savanna sub-Jovians with similar radii and masses, HD 148797 is also a promising target for comparative atmospheric characterisation.
