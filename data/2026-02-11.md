### [1] [Limits on the Post-eclipse Emission Spectrum of HD 80606 b From High-Resolution Spectroscop](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.09129)
*Luke Finnerty,Aurora Kesseli,Kyle Pearson,Charles Beichman,Michael P. Fitzgerald*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 利用Keck/NIRSPEC对高偏心率系外行星HD 80606 b进行近红外观测，在近星点附近获得初步探测信号，但未发现强热反转，与JWST结果一致。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究高偏心率系外行星HD 80606 b在近星点附近的大气特性，特别是快速加热可能导致的极端化学变化和热反转现象。

Method: 使用Keck/NIRSPEC进行K波段观测，采用高分辨率互相关光谱分析（HRCCS）技术，并进行大气反演分析，包括自由P-T剖面和匹配JWST结果的剖面。

Result: 获得初步探测信号（SNR~4），排除了强热反转的存在，反演分析表明可能存在CH₄和CO的吸收特征，与JWST观测结果一致。

Conclusion: HD 80606 b未在数据中被明确探测到，结果与JWST观测一致，未来需要更高光谱分辨率和/或更宽波长覆盖的观测才能通过高分辨率光谱学获得可靠的大气探测。

Abstract: We present Keck/NIRSPEC $K$-band observations of HD 80606 b, one of the most eccentric known exoplanets. HD 80606 b was observed after secondary eclipse, close to periastron, when the planet passes within 0.03 AU of HD 80606 and the rapid heating of the atmosphere may lead to extreme chemical changes and a temporary thermal inversion. The rapid change in the planetary radial velocity near periastron is sufficient to enable high-resolution cross-correlation spectroscopy (HRCCS) analysis, which produces a tentative detection ($\rm SNR\sim4$) of HD 80606 b. Injection-recovery tests appear to reject strong thermal inversions near periastron, consistent with recent results from JWST. We also perform atmospheric retrievals with free parameters for the Pressure-Temperature ($P-T$) profile and with a profile matched to the JWST results, which suggest the presence of absorption features from CH$_4$ and CO. While HD 80606 b is not definitively detected in these data, these results are consistent with JWST observations, which found the post-eclipse atmosphere of HD 80606 b shows weak absorption features from these species. Future observations with higher spectral resolution and/or wider wavelength coverage are needed for a confident atmospheric detection of HD 80606 b via high-resolution spectroscopy alone, but such observations are a challenge to schedule due to the 111-day orbital period.

### [2] [Origin of Hyperion and Saturn's Rings in A Two-Stage Saturnian System Instability](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.09281)
*Matija Ćuk,Maryame El Moutamid,Jim Fuller,Valéry Lainey*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 土星环和部分卫星的年龄是个未解之谜，证据指向数百Myr前的一次灾变事件。研究提出土星最大卫星土卫六的潮汐扩张驱动系统演化，土星倾角和土卫七轨道记录了土卫六的过去演化。土卫七形成于土星自转共振打破时，原土卫七与土卫六碰撞产生碎片形成土卫七，同时激发了土卫八轨道倾角和土卫六偏心率，导致内卫星系统不稳定、碰撞瓦解和重聚，包括土星环的形成。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 解决土星环和部分卫星年龄的未解之谜，解释观测到的土星系统特征（土星倾角、土卫七轨道、土卫八轨道倾角等），提出一个统一的演化模型来解释这些现象。

Method: 提出理论模型：土卫六的潮汐扩张驱动系统演化，导致土星自转共振打破，原土卫七与土卫六碰撞形成土卫七。通过数值模拟验证这一系列事件链的概率和可行性。

Result: 数值模拟显示这一系列事件链具有相对较高的概率，能够解释土星系统的多个观测特征：土星倾角、土卫七与土卫六的轨道共振、土卫八的轨道倾角、土卫六的偏心率激发，以及内卫星系统（包括土星环）的破坏和重聚。

Conclusion: 土星系统的演化由土卫六的潮汐扩张驱动，数百Myr前的灾变事件（原土卫七与土卫六碰撞）解释了土卫七的形成、土卫八轨道倾角、土卫六偏心率激发，并最终导致内卫星系统（包括土星环）的破坏和重聚。这一模型与土星系统的观测特征相符。

Abstract: The age of the rings and some of the moons of Saturn is an open question, and multiple lines of evidence point to a recent (few hundred Myr ago) cataclysm involving disruption of past moons. The main driver of the evolution of the Saturnian system is the relatively rapid tidal expansion of its largest moon, Titan, which is likely driven by resonant tides within Saturn. The obliquity of Saturn and the orbit of the small moon Hyperion both serve as a record of the past orbital evolution of Titan. Saturn's obliquity was likely generated by a secular spin-orbit resonance with the planets, while Hyperion is caught in a mean-motion resonance with Titan, with both phenomena driven by Titan's orbital expansion. We propose that the breaking of Saturn's spin resonance was also the event in which Hyperion formed, when an outer mid-sized satellite (``Proto-Hyperion'') was destabilized and collided with Titan, with some of the debris accreting into Hyperion. During the instability Proto-Hyperion's perturbations produced the observed orbital inclination of Iapetus. The same event also excited the eccentricity of Titan, which then, through Titan's resonant interaction with the inner moons, led to destabilization, collisional disruption and re-accretion of the inner moon system, including the rings. We present numerical integrations that show that this chain of events has a relatively high probability, and discuss how it fits within our knowledge of the Saturnian system.

### [3] [The compositions of the HR 8799 planets reflect accretion of both solids and metal-enriched gas](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.09422)
*Jerry W. Xuan,Jean-Baptiste Ruffio,Yayaati Chachan,Kazumasa Ohno,Aurora Y. Kesseli,Ruth A. Murray-Clay,Eve J. Lee,Julianne I. Moses,William O. Balmer,Aneesh Baburaj,Geoffrey A. Blake,Doug Johnstone,Yapeng Zhang,Heather A. Knutson,Dimitri Mawet,Charles Beichman,Klaus W. Hodapp,Marshall D. Perrin,Quinn M. Konopacky,Michael R. Meyer,Geoffrey Bryden,Thomas P. Greene,Jarron Leisenring,Marie Ygouf,Björn Benneke,Julie Inglis,Nicole L. Wallack*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: JWST观测HR 8799系统四颗巨行星的大气成分，发现碳氧丰度相似但硫丰度随轨道距离增加，表明外行星吸积更多固态物质，支持卵石和星子吸积模型。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: HR 8799系统拥有四颗巨行星，为研究巨行星形成和原行星盘成分梯度提供了独特机会。通过JWST观测这些行星的大气成分，可以揭示它们的形成历史和吸积过程。

Method: 使用JWST/NIRSpec IFU观测HR 8799系统的四颗行星（2.85-5.3μm波段），获得行星b的新光谱和其他行星更高信噪比的光谱。结合1-5μm测光数据，进行大气反演分析，考虑非平衡化学和云层影响，独立标定C/H、O/H、N/H和S/H丰度。

Result: 检测到CO、CH₄、H₂O、H₂S、CO₂，行星b还检测到NH₃。四颗行星的碳氧丰度相似（3-5倍恒星值），但硫丰度随轨道距离增加而增加（2-5倍恒星值）。行星b的氮丰度显著偏高（21.2倍恒星值），表明吸积了富氮气体。总体丰度模式支持行星b在CO雪线和N₂雪线之间形成，内行星在CO雪线内吸积富CO气体。

Conclusion: 观测到的元素丰度模式与行星形成理论一致：硫丰度梯度表明外行星吸积更多固态物质，卵石通量估计为750±200M⊕。这些结果与卵石吸积和星子吸积模型（2倍最小太阳星云）兼容，为巨行星形成机制提供了重要约束。

Abstract: With four giant planets ($m\sim5-10~M_{\rm Jup}$, $T_\rm{eff}\sim900-1200$ K) orbiting between 15-70 au, HR 8799 provides an unparalleled testbed for studying giant planet formation and probing compositional trends across the protoplanetary disk. We present new JWST/NIRSpec IFU observations ($2.85-5.3~μ$m, $R\approx2700$) that now include the spectrum of HR 8799 b, and higher S/N spectra for HR 8799 c, d, and e compared to that in Ruffio & Xuan et al. 2026. We detect CO, CH$_4$, H$_2$O, H$_2$S, CO$_2$, and for planet b, NH$_3$. We combine the NIRSpec spectra with $1-5 μ$m photometry to perform atmospheric retrievals that account for disequilibrium chemistry and clouds, and allow C/H, O/H, N/H, and S/H to scale independently. While the four planets are similarly enriched in carbon and oxygen, with C/H and O/H between $3-5\times$ stellar, we observe a tentative trend of increasing S/H - a tracer of refractory solids - from $2-5 \times$ stellar with increasing orbital distance. From HR 8799 b's NH$_3$ abundance, we estimate $\rm N/H=21.2^{+16.2}_{-8.8}\times$ stellar, suggesting the outer planet accreted significant amounts of N-rich gas. Overall, the elemental abundance patterns we observe are consistent with a picture where planet b formed between the CO snowline and the more-distant N$_2$ snowline, while the inner planets accreted $3 \times$ stellar CO-enriched disk gas within the CO snowline. The excess volatile mass from pebble drift and evaporation implies an integrated pebble flux of $750 \pm 200~M_{\oplus}$. The increase in the planets' S/H with orbital distance implies more solid accretion further out, which is quantitatively compatible with expectations from both pebble and planetesimal accretion ($2 \times$ Minimum Mass Solar Nebula) paradigms.

### [4] [A Narrowband Technosignature Search Toward the Hycean Candidate K2-18b Using the VLA and MeerKAT](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.09553)
*C. D. Tremblay,S. Chaudhary,Megan G. Li,Sofia Z. Sheikh,T. Myburgh,D. Czech,D. E. MacMahon,P. B. Demorest,R. A. Donnachie,A. P. V. Siemion,V. Gajjar,M. Lebofsky,K. Wandia,K. I. Perez.,Nikku Madhusudhan*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 使用VLA和MeerKAT望远镜对K2-18b进行多波段、多历元射电技术信号搜索，未检测到任何天体物理或人工信号，为Hycean行星候选体首次提供了干涉测量技术信号限制。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: K2-18b是一颗位于恒星宜居带内的亚海王星系外行星，JWST观测表明其可能具有富氢大气层，符合Hycean世界特征，因此成为评估潜在宜居性和技术信号的重要目标。

Method: 使用VLA（COSMIC后端）和MeerKAT（BLUSE后端）进行协调多历元观测，覆盖544MHz至9.8GHz频率范围，包含至少一个完整行星轨道周期。开发了综合后处理框架，包括RFI掩蔽、基于系统动力学的漂移率滤波、多波束空间鉴别、主次凌星滤波以及基于信噪比的虚假信号剔除。

Conclusion: 研究证明了协调多历元干涉测量搜索的有效性，为未来对附近潜在宜居系外行星的技术信号研究建立了方法论框架，尽管未检测到信号，但为类似目标的技术信号搜索提供了重要参考。

Abstract: K2-18b, a sub-Neptune exoplanet located in the habitable zone of its host star, has emerged as an important target for atmospheric characterization and assessments of potential habitability. Motivated by recent interpretations of JWST observations suggesting a hydrogen-rich atmosphere consistent with Hycean-world scenarios, we conducted a coordinated, multi-epoch search for narrowband radio technosignatures using the Karl G. Jansky Very Large Array equipped with the COSMIC backend and the MeerKAT telescope with the BLUSE backend. Our observations span frequencies from 544MHz to 9.8GHz and include multiple epochs that cover at least one full orbital period of the planet. In this work, we outline, create, and apply a comprehensive post-processing framework that incorporates observatory-informed RFI masking, drift-rate filtering based on the expected dynamics of the K2-18 system, multibeam spatial discrimination, primary and secondary transit filtering (when applicable), and SNR-based excision of weak and strong spurious signals. Across all bands and epochs, no signals consistent with an astrophysical or artificial origin were identified at a limit of 10^12 to 10^13W. These non-detections allow us to place upper limits on the presence of persistent, isotropic narrowband transmitters within the K2-18 system, providing the first interferometric technosignature constraints for a Hycean-planet candidate. Our results demonstrate the efficacy of coordinated multiepoch interferometric searches and establish a methodological framework for future technosignature studies of nearby potentially habitable exoplanets.

### [5] [Comet 73P/Schwassmann-Wachmann 3's Dust Trail as a Source of Pickup Ions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.09601)
*Samuel R. Grant,Geraint H. Jones,Antoinette B. Galvin*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 该研究分析了彗星73P/Schwassmann-Wachmann 3的碎片在2006年和2011年期间产生的彗星离子尾迹，通过太阳风速度测量和三维外推技术，发现即使彗星碎片与航天器对齐不佳，仍能在扩散的离子尾迹中检测到彗星离子，表明彗星碎片延伸轨迹是重要的彗星离子源。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究彗星73P/Schwassmann-Wachmann 3多次分裂事件产生的碎片如何通过太阳风将彗星离子输送到地球附近，并探索在彗星碎片与航天器对齐不佳的情况下，彗星离子尾迹的扩散特性及其对空间环境的影响。

Method: 利用ACE、Wind和STEREO-B航天器的观测数据，结合太阳风速度测量进行三维外推，计算太阳风流线，比较已知彗星和彗星碎片的位置，估计彗星离子尾迹与航天器的分离距离。

Result: 尽管主要彗星碎片与航天器的对齐条件不利于彗星离子通过太阳风直接输送，但航天器仍能沉浸在围绕延伸尘埃轨迹的扩散离子尾迹中，检测到来自彗星碎片73P-AM的彗星离子，表明在较远距离下，彗星碎片延伸轨迹是重要的彗星离子源。

Conclusion: 彗星73P的延伸碎片轨迹在距离彗核较远时仍能产生显著的彗星离子尾迹，即使对齐条件不理想，扩散的离子尾迹仍能被航天器检测到，这对理解彗星物质在太阳系中的分布和输送机制具有重要意义。

Abstract: 73P/Schwassmann-Wachmann 3 is a short-period comet that has undergone multiple fragmentation events in the last few decades. During May-June 2006, while passing near Earth, multiple fragments of comet 73P passed sunward of Sun-Earth Lagrange Point 1, while cometary pickup ions were detected concurrently by instruments on both the ACE and Wind spacecraft, implying the crossing of one or more ion tail. Additionally, during August 2011, a fragment of 73P passed directly sunward of spacecraft STEREO-B. A detection of cometary ions is shown to originate at fragment 73P-AM. Solar wind velocity measurements are used to extrapolate the flow of the solar wind in 3 dimensions and, when compared with the positions of known comets and cometary fragments, estimate the separation between the cometary ion tail and the spacecraft. Using this technique, it is shown that the alignment of the major cometary fragments with the spacecraft was poor for the transport of cometary ions via the solar wind, but the encounter was near enough for immersion in the diffuse ion tail surrounding an extended dust trail within which the nucleus fragments reside. This implies that, at this distance, the extended trail of cometary debris was a significant source of cometary ions in the case of comet 73P.
