### [1] [The Surface and Interior Conditions of Temperate Sub-Neptune TOI-270 d](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.16722)
*Frances E. Rigby,Nikku Madhusudhan*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 该论文通过耦合大气-内部结构模型，探索了亚海王星TOI-270 d可能的内部结构范围，包括迷你海王星、气体矮星和Hycean情景，并分析了其表面宜居条件。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 亚海王星在太阳系中没有对应物，对理解系外行星多样性、形成演化和宜居性具有重要意义。TOI-270 d的JWST观测数据为研究其内部结构提供了机会。

Method: 使用耦合的大气-内部结构模型进行内部结构建模，包括基于JWST观测的自洽大气温度结构，考虑不同情景下的表面条件和大气性质。

Result: 发现TOI-270 d可能具有多种内部结构，包括迷你海王星、气体矮星和Hycean情景。宜居表面条件需要大气质量分数≲3.5×10⁻⁵，压力≲100巴。气体矮星情景需要H₂富集包层质量分数约1-5%，表面压力约10⁴-10⁵巴。

Conclusion: TOI-270 d的内部结构和表面条件存在多种可能性，需要进一步的理论、实验研究和大气观测来准确表征其内部和表面条件。

Abstract: Sub-Neptune planets, with no analogue in our solar system, provide a wealth of information about exoplanet diversity, formation & evolution, and habitability. Their robust characterisation requires the coupling of physically informed atmosphere and interior models with precise atmospheric data to break compositional degeneracies. Recent JWST observations of the temperate sub-Neptune TOI-270 d revealed detections of CH$_4$ and CO$_2$ in its H$_2$-rich atmosphere, with tentative inferences of H$_2$O and CS$_2$ and a non-detection of NH$_3$. We conduct a theoretical exploration of the range of possible interiors for TOI-270 d based on the current observational constraints. We carry out internal structure modelling using a coupled atmosphere-interior model, including self-consistent atmospheric temperature structures informed by JWST observations. The bulk properties permit solutions spanning mini-Neptune, gas dwarf and hycean scenarios, with a wide range of possible surface conditions, which are strongly dependent on the atmospheric properties, including the presence of clouds/hazes. We explore the solutions allowing for surface water oceans on TOI-270 d, including under potentially habitable conditions. The atmospheric mass fractions permitting habitable surface conditions are found to be $\lesssim$$3.5\times10^{-5}$ and pressures $\lesssim$100 bar for the envelope temperature structures considered. We consider mini-Neptune interiors that are sufficiently warm for H$_2$O to be mixed with the H$_2$-rich envelope. Finally, we consider possible gas dwarf interiors, finding H$_2$-rich envelope mass fractions of $\sim$$1-5$ % are required to satisfy the bulk properties, with surface pressures $\sim10^4-10^5$ bar. Further theoretical and experimental studies in addition to future atmospheric observations will aid the characterisation of the possible interior and surface conditions on TOI-270 d.

### [2] [Zooming into the water snowline: high resolution water observations of the HL Tau disk](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.16737)
*M. Leemker,S. Facchini,P. Curone,L. Rampinelli,M. Benisty,A. Garufi,E. Humphreys*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 使用ALMA高分辨率观测解析HL Tau原行星盘中的水、H$^{13}$CO$^+$和SO发射，确定水雪线位置在中央6 au以内，并发现ALMA能观测到35-70%的水储层，而中远红外只能看到0.02%-2%。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 水是行星形成和宜居性的关键分子，水雪线区域可能是原行星盘中形成行星的有利位置，需要精确确定水雪线位置并理解水储层的分布。

Method: 使用ALMA高分辨率观测HL Tau盘中的H$_2$O 183 GHz、H$^{13}$CO$^+$和SO发射线，通过旋转图分析表征水储层，并与中远红外波段的观测进行比较。

Result: 发现水雪线位于中央6 au以内，水激发温度约350 K且与半径无关；ALMA能观测到35-70%的水储层，而中远红外只能看到0.02%-2%；发现H$_2$O与H$^{13}$CO$^+$发射呈反相关；首次在SO中观测到分子外流和吸积流。

Conclusion: 亚毫米波段的水线空间分辨观测为水雪线位置提供了宝贵约束，同时探测了大部分气相储层，这对理解行星形成过程具有重要意义。

Abstract: Water is one of the central molecules for the formation and habitability of planets. In particular, the region where water freezes-out, the water snowline, could be a favorable location to form planets in protoplanetary disks. We use high resolution ALMA observations to spatially resolve H$_2$O, H$^{13}$CO$^+$ and SO emission in the HL Tau disk. A rotational diagram analysis is used to characterize the water reservoir seen with ALMA and compare this to the reservoir visible at mid- and far-IR wavelengths. We find that the H$_2$O 183 GHz line has a compact central component and a diffuse component that is seen out to ~75 au. A radially resolved rotational diagram shows that the excitation temperature of the water is ~350 K independent of radius. The steep drop in the water brightness temperature outside the central beam of the observations where the emission is optically thick is consistent with the water snowline being located inside the central beam ($\lesssim 6$ au) at the height probed by the observations. Comparing the ALMA lines to those seen at shorter wavelengths shows that only 0.02%-2% of the water reservoir is visible at mid- and far-IR wavelengths, respectively, due to optically thick dust hiding the emission whereas 35-70% is visible with ALMA. An anti-correlation between the H$_2$O and H$^{13}$CO$^+$ emission is found but this is likely caused by optically thick dust hiding the H$^{13}$CO$^+$ emission in the disk center. Finally, we see SO emission tracing the disk and for the first time in SO a molecular outflow and the infalling streamer out to ~2". The velocity structure hints at a possible connection between the SO and the H$_2$O emission. Spatially resolved observations of H$_2$O lines at (sub-)mm wavelengths provide valuable constraints on the location of the water snowline, while probing the bulk of the gas-phase reservoirs.

### [4] [A Comprehensive Analysis of the Panchromatic Transmission Spectrum of the Hot-Saturn WASP-96 b: Nondetection of Haze, Possible Sodium Limb Asymmetry, Stellar Characterization, and Formation History](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.16771)
*Le-Chris Wang,Zafar Rustamkulov,David K. Sing,Joshua Lothringer,Patrick McCreery,Daniel Thorngren,Munazza K. Alam*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 对WASP-96 b的重新分析显示，之前JWST观测中发现的瑞利散射斜率并不存在，表明大气中没有高空气溶胶。研究发现存在灰色云层，但位于较低高度。通过结合多台望远镜数据，约束了多种化学物种的质量分数和行星内部结构。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 重新分析JWST对WASP-96 b的观测数据，验证之前发现的瑞利散射斜率是否真实存在，并更准确地表征该系外行星的大气成分和形成历史。

Method: 重新分析JWST NIRISS/SOSS观测数据，并结合VLT、HST和Spitzer的数据，构建从0.35μm到5μm的透射光谱。使用大气模型约束化学成分，并将成分约束输入内部模型。

Result: 未检测到瑞利散射斜率，确认大气中无高空气溶胶。发现存在灰色云层（低高度/高压）。约束了H2O、K、Na等化学物种的质量分数，C/O比和金属丰度为亚恒星值。内部模型显示核心质量约43地球质量。钠吸收特征显示大气不对称性。

Conclusion: WASP-96 b的核心可能形成于水冰线之外，经历了盘驱动迁移，并在碳烟线内吸积了大气。碳系物种（CH4、CO、CO2）仍受光谱覆盖限制，需要未来的JWST观测来约束。

Abstract: We conduct a reanalysis of the JWST NIRISS/SOSS observation of the hot-Saturn WASP-96 b. Initial analysis of this data revealed an enhanced Rayleigh scattering slope at the blue end of the transmission spectrum, suggesting the presence of hazes at high altitudes. In this work, we report non-detection of this slope, confirming an atmosphere clear of high-altitude aerosols consistent with the pre-JWST results. Also contrary to the initial result, our results indicate the presence of gray cloud deck, although at relatively low altitudes/high pressures. We further combined the NIRISS/SOSS spectrum with VLT, HST, and Spitzer to produce a transmission spectrum from 0.35 $μ$m to 5 $μ$m. We constrain the mass fraction of multiple chemical species, including: H$_2$O$=-2.62^{+0.43}_{-0.42}$, K$=-5.76^{+1.05}_{-1.13}$, and Na$=-3.40^{+0.90}_{-0.92}$. Inputing these composition constraints to interior models, we constrain a core mass of $43^{+8}_{-15}$ M$_\oplus$. This, in addition to our inferred super-stellar refractory-to-oxygen ratio ($Δ\log_{10}(R/O)=1.48^{+0.57}_{-0.62}$) and substellar C/O ratio, suggests that the core of WASP-96 b likely formed outside of water iceline, underwent disk-driven migration, and accreted its atmosphere inside the carbon soot line. We find evidence of atmospheric leading-trailing terminator asymmetries in the broadened sodium absorption feature with a transit time offset of 50 seconds, while the water features appear symmetric. CH$_4$, CO, and CO$_2$ remain unconstrained due to spectral coverage limits. Upcoming JWST NIRSpec/G395H observations (ID 4082, PI: M. Radica) will be crucial for constraining these carbon-bearing species.

### [5] [Constructing Earth Formation History Using Deep Mantle Noble Gas Reservoirs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.16859)
*Vincent Savignac,Eve J. Lee*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 地球早期形成始于约0.3地球质量的胚胎，在太阳星云消散期间通过吸积原始气体并将氖溶解到岩浆海洋中，这解释了地幔深部的原始氖同位素特征。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 通过研究地幔深部氖同位素比值来理解地球早期形成过程，特别是原始星云气体如何被纳入地球内部。

Method: 模拟原始包层的生长过程，使用现代气体吸积方案，并计算星云氖在化学平衡条件下溶解到岩浆海洋中的过程。

Result: 发现能够重现地幔深部原始氖浓度的胚胎质量严格限制在约0.3地球质量，且需要太阳星云气体密度减少至少100倍。较小胚胎无法吸积足够气体使地幔达到玄武岩熔点，较大胚胎则吸积过多气体导致氖浓度过高。

Conclusion: 地球形成始于约0.3地球质量胚胎在太阳星云消散期间的组装，轻贵气体反映地球原始气体吸积历史，重贵气体反映早期固体吸积过程，结果与地球通过至少两次巨大撞击完成最终组装的观点一致。

Abstract: Noble gases are powerful probes of the Earth's early history, as they are chemically inert. Neon isotopic ratios in deep mantle plumes suggest that nebular gases were incorporated into the Earth's interior. This evidence implies the Earth's formation began when there was still gas around, with Earth embryos accreting primordial gas and a fraction of that gas dissolved into molten magma. In this work, we examine these implications, simulating the growth of primordial envelopes using modern gas accretion schemes, and computing the dissolution of nebular Ne into magma oceans following chemical equilibrium. We find that the embryo mass that reproduces the deep mantle concentration of primordial Ne is tightly constrained to $\sim 0.3 M_\oplus$, within a solar nebula depleted by $\geq 100 \times$ in gas density. Embryos of smaller masses cannot accrete enough gas to allow the mantle to reach the melting temperature of basalt. Embryos of larger masses accrete way too much gas, producing excessive Ne concentrations in the deep mantle. Based on our calculations, we suggest that the Earth's formation began with the assembly of $\sim 0.3 M_\oplus$ embryos during the dispersal of the solar nebula. Light noble gases (He, Ne) in the deep mantle reflect the primordial gas accretion history of the Earth, while heavy noble gases (Ar, Kr, Xe) probe early solid accretion processes. Our results are consistent with the final assembly of the Earth through at least two giant impacts after the dispersal of the nebula.

</details>
