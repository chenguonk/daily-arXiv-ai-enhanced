### [1] [A Massive Hot-Jupiter Companion that Disfavors Giant Planet Formation Beyond the Water-Ice Line](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02705)
*Eritas Yang,Tiger Lu,Daniel A. Yahalomi,Joshua N. Winn*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 在KELT-20系统中发现一颗褐矮星伴星（质量34+30-11 M_J），通过天体测量加速度和凌星时间变化联合分析推断其性质。该伴星轨道对热木星形成位置施加动力学限制，结果不支持冰线外形成，而倾向于较小距离形成后向内迁移。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探究热木星（KELT-20b）在存在大质量伴星（褐矮星）的系统中的形成位置，检验其是否可能形成于水冰线之外并通过迁移到达当前轨道。

Method: 联合分析天体测量加速度（astrometric accelerations）和凌星时间变化（transit timing variations, TTVs）来推断褐矮星伴星的质量和轨道参数，并基于轨道动力学分析评估热木星的可能形成区域。

Result: 褐矮星近日点距离仅几天文单位，导致任何在冰线（~8-15 au）附近形成的行星都会发生轨道交叉或长期不稳定。若伴星一直处于当前轨道，原热木星必须在~3.7 au以内形成以避免轨道交叉，且在~1.5 au以内才能保持长期动力学稳定。

Conclusion: 结果不利于热木星在冰线外形成，支持其在较小轨道距离形成后经历向内迁移的演化路径。

Abstract: We report evidence for a brown-dwarf companion with mass $34^{+30}_{-11}~M_{\rm J}$ in the KELT-20 system, in which an ultra-hot Jupiter transits an A2-type star. The companion's properties are inferred from a joint analysis of astrometric accelerations and transit timing variations, and its present-day orbit imposes dynamical limits on where the hot Jupiter could have formed. Given the star's current luminosity, the water-ice line is expected at $\sim$8-15 au, but the companion's inferred pericenter distance of a few au would lead to orbit crossing or long-term instability for any planet formed at such distances. If the companion formed early and remained near its current orbit over the system's lifetime, the proto-hot Jupiter must have formed within $\sim$3.7 au to avoid orbit crossing, and within $\sim$1.5 au to remain dynamically stable over the system's lifetime. These results disfavor formation beyond the ice line and point instead to formation at smaller orbital distances followed by inward migration.

### [2] [Formation of multiple dust rings and gaps in protoplanetary discs by a single migrating planet II: radiative discs and observational signatures](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02734)
*Kim M. Weiskopf,Sören C. Meiners,Alexandros Ziampras,Cornelis P. Dullemond*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本文通过高分辨率二维流体动力学模拟，研究了一颗在低粘度、含辐射冷却的原行星盘中迁移的行星能否产生可观测的尘埃结构。结果显示，无论采用何种状态方程，行星都会经历一次或多次迁移跳跃，每次跳跃都会产生尘埃环和间隙。这些结构至少能保持可见400 kyr，但大尺度涡旋平均仅持续90 kyr。冷却模型对迁移行为有决定性影响，但对尘埃结构的寿命无显著影响。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 原行星盘中的尘埃结构已被广泛观测，但其形成机制仍是活跃研究领域。已有研究探索了磁流体动力学、阴影和行星-盘相互作用等可能起源。本文旨在探究一颗在低粘度盘中（含辐射过程）迁移的行星是否足以产生可观测的尘埃结构，并考察这些结构的寿命和潜在不对称性。

Method: 使用高分辨率二维流体动力学模拟，对一颗迁移行星采用三种不同的状态方程：等温、常数β冷却以及自适应β模型。所有模拟中均包含尘埃，并对生成的尘埃密度分布进行后处理，生成辐射传输图像。

Result: 所有状态方程下，行星均经历一次或多次迁移跳跃，每次跳跃产生尘埃环和间隙。尘埃结构至少可见400 kyr，大尺度涡旋平均仅持续90 kyr。冷却模型对迁移行为和跳跃次数有决定性影响，但对尘埃结构寿命无显著影响。结构呈现的不对称性较少。

Conclusion: 模型突显了行星在迁移过程中打开多个间隙的能力，并强调了使用真实冷却模型的重要性。在直接解释和比较此类模型与观测结果时应谨慎。

Abstract: Dust structures in protoplanetary discs have been widely observed and their creation remains an active field of research. Several possible origins have already been explored, including magneto-hydrodynamics, shadows and planets-disc interactions. The goal of this paper is to investigate whether a single migrating planet in a low-viscosity disc, including radiative processes, is capable of generating observable dust structures. We aim to examine both the lifetime of such structures and potential asymmetries within them. We perform a set of high-resolution, two-dimensional hydrodynamic simulations of migrating planets using three different equations of state: isothermal, constant $β$-cooling and an adaptive $β$ model. Dust is included in all simulations and the resulting dust density profiles are then post-processed to create radiative transfer images. For all equations of state considered, the planet undergoes one or several migration jumps, each producing dust rings and gaps. The lifetime of these structures depends on the phase of slow migration preceding and occurring between jumps, but in all cases they remain visible for at least 400 kyr. We find that cooling has a deciding effect on the migration behaviour and the number of jumps, but no measurable influence on the lifetime of the dust structures. The structures exhibit relatively few asymmetries, and large-scale vortices persist for an average of only 90 kyr. Our models highlight the capacity of planets to open multiple gaps while migrating, and stress the importance of a realistic cooling model. Care should be taken when interpreting and comparing such models directly to observations.

### [3] [A comprehensive Rossiter-Mclaughlin Modelling Framework in TLCM: Application to HD 2685 $=$ TOI-135 system](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.03456)
*Szilárd Csizmadia,Alexis M. S. Smith,J. V. Harre,Gábor G. Balázs*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本文在TLCM代码中更新了Rossiter-McLaughlin建模框架，并通过9个已知系统和一颗新星HD 2685（TOI-135）进行了验证，测量了其自旋-轨道夹角λ=55.6°。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 改进Rossiter-McLaughlin效应的建模框架，以更精确地测量系外行星系统的自旋-轨道倾角，从而理解行星形成和迁移的演化历史。

Method: 在TLCM代码中更新了RM效应建模框架；利用TESS测光和归档径向速度数据验证了9个已知系统；对HD 2685获取了新的HARPS径向速度数据和额外TESS测光数据，进行参数精化和RM效应探测。

Result: 成功验证了模型在9个系统上的有效性；在HD 2685中探测到Rossiter-McLaughlin效应，得到其投影自旋-轨道夹角λ=55.6°（+10.9°/-11.9°），且该中心值对不同的V sin I⋆先验保持稳定。

Conclusion: 更新的RM建模框架有效且稳定；HD 2685是一个中等倾斜度的热木星系统，表明其可能存在倾斜的迁移历史。

Abstract: We present an updated Rossiter-McLaughlin modelling framework in the exoplanet analysis code \textsc{TLCM}. We describe our model in detail. The model and the code were validated by nine systems where we use TESS photometric measurements and archive radial velocity data (WASP-15, HAT-P-1, HAT-P-3, HAT-P-6, HAT-P-7, HAT-P-11, HAT-P-14, HAT-P-20, HAT-P-32). In addition, new observations were obtained from HD 2685 = TOI-135 which is an evolved, hot ($T_\mathrm{eff}=6801$ K), single star hosting a $\sim 1.15$ Jupiter-mass transiting hot Jupiter in a $\sim 4.1$ days orbit. We obtained new HARPS radial velocity measurements in-transit and out-of-transit. Also, there are new photometric observations from six additional, yet not-analyzed TESS-sectors since the time of the discovery of its transits. This allowed us to refine the planetary, orbital and system parameters and to detect Rossiter-Mclaughlin effect in it. We find an intermediate sky-projected obliquity, $λ= \ang{55.6}^{+\ang{10.9}}_{-\ang{11.9}}$, for our fiducial spectroscopic \(V\sin I_\star\) prior. Tests with broader \(V\sin I_\star\) priors show that the central value of \(λ\) remains stable, although the uncertainty increases.

</details>
