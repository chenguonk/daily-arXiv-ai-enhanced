### [1] [Extreme Debris Disks: Insights into Violent Collisions in Planet Formation and Destruction](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06684)
*Kate Y. L. Su,Attila Moor,Agnes Kospal,George H. Rieke,Antranik A. Sefilian,Renu Malhotra,Ilaria Pascucci,Alan P. Jackson,Peter Abraham,Nicholas P. Ballering*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 本文分析了21个极端碎片盘（EDDs）的JWST和Spitzer中红外光谱，发现这些盘含有大量光学薄、亚微米级的热蚀变尘埃颗粒（富含硅石和结晶硅酸盐），并呈现随机红外变异性，表明这些尘埃来自月球至火星大小的天体间剧烈碰撞，为类地行星形成过程中的暴力事件条件提供了诊断信息，并可作为行星系统经历动力学不稳定的潜在标记。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探究极端碎片盘（EDDs）中由撞击产生的尘埃矿物学特征与这些系统在不同行星形成和演化阶段的年龄及动力学状态之间的联系。

Method: 对21个EDDs进行JWST和Spitzer中红外光谱分析。

Result: EDDs含有比原行星盘和普通碎片盘显著更多的光学薄、小尘埃颗粒；这些颗粒主要为亚微米级，经过热蚀变，富含硅石和结晶硅酸盐；呈现随机红外变异性。

Conclusion: EDDs是碎片盘的一个子类，其尘埃来自月球至火星大小天体的剧烈碰撞；这些特征可作为识别经历动力学不稳定的行星系统的潜在标记，并为类地行星形成过程中的撞击事件提供诊断信息。

Abstract: Debris disks are dusty structures around mature stars, primarily identified by infrared excesses in the stellar spectra. A subset, known as extreme debris disks (EDDs), exhibits stochastic infrared variability, believed to result from large-scale violent collisions that contribute to the formation or destruction of rocky planetary bodies. We analyze JWST and Spitzer mid-infrared spectra for 21 EDDs to investigate the connection between impact-produced dust mineralogy and the age and dynamical state of these systems during various stages of planet formation and evolution. Our findings indicate that EDDs contain significantly more optically thin, small dust grains compared to those typically seen in protoplanetary and debris disks. Predominantly submicron in size, these grains are thermally altered, as shown by their high levels of silica and crystalline silicate composition. Along with stochastic infrared variability, these features define EDDs as a subclass of debris systems where dust is generated from large collisions between Moon- and Mars-sized bodies. Our results not only provide diagnostic information about the physical conditions of violent events during terrestrial planet formation, aiding in differentiating the complex outcomes of these impacts, but also offer a potential marker for identifying planetary systems experiencing dynamical instability.

Abstract: We present the first panchromatic JWST transmission spectrum of an ultra-hot Jupiter, combining NIRISS and NIRSpec observations to constrain KELT-7\,b's atmospheric properties. This ``late-ramp-fit'' method accurately calculates KELT-7\,b's transmission spectrum between 1--1.5~$μ$m at higher signal-to-noise. Unaccounted-for systematics may therefore bias longer-wavelength WFC3/IR G141 transit depths shallower. Our free retrieval, two equilibrium retrievals, and self-consistent grid fit all prefer a high metallicity but find discrepant C/O ratios. Agglomerated together, we constrain a super-stellar $\text{M/H}=92^{+24}_{-23}\times$~Solar and C/O~$\leq0.9$, suggesting enhanced metal accretion in the later stages of KELT-7\,b's formation. Our GCMs explain the observed lack of limb asymmetry with superrotating jet-driven efficient horizontal mixing. The stark contrast between our panchromatic analysis and prior analyses on subsets of these data demonstrates the value of broad wavelength coverage for the comprehensive study of exoplanet atmospheres.

### [6] [Radio emission from close-in exoplanets: Can we extend the radio-magnetic scaling law to the sub-Alfvénic stellar wind regime?](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07704)

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 尽管基于太阳系行星的无线电-磁场缩放定律（RMSL）预测系外行星应有可探测的射电极光，但实际观测一直未得到确认。作者质疑该定律是否适用于处于更强磁场、亚阿尔文风环境下的近距离系外行星，旨在检验该关系是否能扩展至亚阿尔文恒星风条件。

The RMSL is based on solar system planets in the super-Alvenic solar wind and it is unclear whether the RMSL holds for close-in exoplanets in more magnetic, sub-Alfvenic winds.
